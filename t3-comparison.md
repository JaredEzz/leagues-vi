# Tier 3 Comparison: Map of Alacrity vs Evil Eye vs Bank Heist

**Pick: Map of Alacrity.** Unique travel coverage for Zeah RC + Hydra + ToA that fairy rings can't replicate. Evil Eye's boss teleports are mostly redundant with existing teleport items.

---

## Map of Alacrity

**Effect:** Teleport to any Agility shortcut across all regions. Ignores Agility level requirements. Ignores wilderness restrictions.

### Where It Matters

| Location | Shortcut | Agility Req (bypassed) | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Alch. Hydra dungeon** | Mysterious pipe (Mount Karuulm) | 88 | **Biggest bossing impact.** Skips most of Karuulm dungeon. Saves ~15-20s per Hydra trip. Farmed repeatedly on task with no direct teleport item. |
| **Zeah RC: Blood Altar → Mine** | Western Rocks | 73 | **Biggest skilling impact.** Teleport to shortcut near Blood Altar, drop into mine. Skips the entire return run. See [runecraft.md](skills/runecraft.md). |
| **Zeah RC: Soul Altar → Mine** | Boulder | 49 | Same for soul runes — teleport near Soul Altar, jump into mine |
| **Zeah RC: Mine ↔ Soul Altar** | Eastern Rocks / Northern Rocks | 52 / 69 | Two-way traversal options for Soul Altar route |
| **ToA (fairy ring AKP)** | Obstacle at AKP fairy ring | 62 | Makes the Necropolis fairy ring usable from day 1 without Agility training |
| **Yama (Chasm of Fire)** | Platform / Chain shortcuts | 73 / 83 | Saves ~10s per trip from fairy ring DJR |
| **Moons of Peril** | Rocks near Cam Torum | 47 | Saves ~10-15s on Ralos' Rise route |
| **KQ** | Crevice inside lair | 86 | Moderate — also needs Elite Desert Diary |

### Zeah RC Deep Dive

The normal Blood/Soul rune loop:
1. Mine dense essence at the mine
2. Run west to Dark Altar → convert blocks
3. Chisel while running
4. Run to Blood Altar (south) or Soul Altar (east) → craft runes
5. **Run all the way back to the mine** ← the slow part

With Map of Alacrity, step 5 becomes: **Teleport to Western Rocks shortcut → drop into mine instantly.** The return run is eliminated.

Combined with the full relic stack:
- **EH:** Possibly 2x dense essence blocks + infinite runestone (no depletion). Needs Day 1 testing — dense runestone is special (Mining cape/Varrock armour don't work on it).
- **Flow State:** Possibly 2-tick mining + 2-tick chiseling. Dense runestone has a fixed 9-tick cycle that may override. Also needs Day 1 testing.
- **Map of Alacrity:** Guaranteed shortcut teleports regardless of other relic interactions.

The ZKT community guide on the wiki explicitly calls out Map of Alacrity for "Zeah RC viability."

### The Pattern

Most bosses fall into one of three categories:

1. **Already have near-instant teleports:** Sol Heredit (Ring of Dueling), Wintertodt (Games necklace), CoX (Xeric's Honour), Hespori (Skills necklace), ToA (Pharaoh's Sceptre). Evil Eye saves <10s. Not worth a relic slot.

2. **Drop their own teleport item within a few kills:** Doom of Mokhaiotl (Waystone, 7/104), Vardorvis (strangled tablet, 1/25.5), Leviathan (scarred tablet, 1/25). Evil Eye is only useful for the first few kills. With 5x drop rates from T4 passive, you'll get these fast.

3. **Genuinely slow to reach:** Sarachnis (~40-60s), Moons of Peril (~30-45s), Hydra (~30-40s without pipe shortcut). These are where Evil Eye would help most. But Hydra is already solved by Map of Alacrity's pipe shortcut, and Sarachnis/Moons aren't high-frequency farms.

## Bank Heist

**Effect:** Teleport to any bank, bank chest, or deposit box. Ignores wilderness restrictions.

**Why it loses:**
- Nature's Accord (T5) fairy mushroom already provides fast bank access via fairy rings to banked areas
- EH auto-banks all gathered resources — banking during gathering is eliminated
- Most boss/Slayer loot goes to Seed Vault or is alched (infinite nats from Transmutation)
- No unique travel coverage that other relics don't provide

## Verdict

| | Map of Alacrity | Evil Eye | Bank Heist |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Zeah RC** | **Eliminates return run** | No benefit | No benefit |
| **Hydra** | **88 Agi pipe shortcut from T3** | Instant teleport | No benefit |
| **ToA access** | **62 Agi fairy ring obstacle bypassed** | Instant teleport | No benefit |
| **Most bosses** | No direct benefit | ~10-25s saved per trip (varies) | No benefit |
| **Bossing frequency** | N/A | Adds up over hundreds of kills | N/A |
| **Unique coverage** | Shortcuts fairy rings can't reach | Boss entrances (most have alt teleports) | Banks (fairy rings cover most) |

**Map of Alacrity wins because:**
1. **RC is a stated hard grind.** Map of Alacrity directly accelerates it. Evil Eye does nothing for RC.
2. **Hydra pipe shortcut is the single best bossing shortcut** — farmed repeatedly on task, no direct teleport item, saves ~15-20s per kill. Map of Alacrity provides this.
3. **Most bosses have existing teleport solutions** that make Evil Eye's savings marginal (5-25s per trip).
4. **Bosses that lack good teleports drop their own** within a few kills (Mokhaiotl Waystone, Ring of Shadows tablets). Evil Eye's value evaporates quickly.
5. **ToA fairy ring (AKP) requires 62 Agility** — Map of Alacrity bypasses this from day 1.

Evil Eye would be the pick for a **pure bossing build** that doesn't care about skilling. For a build that splits time between AFK gathering, active skill grinds (RC, Hunter, Herblore), and bossing, Map of Alacrity provides more total value.
