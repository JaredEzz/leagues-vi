## Demonic Pacts (Combat Masteries — Separate System)

- **40 active pacts at once**, 130+ available
- **3 resets** per league
- Earn pact points from combat tasks (kill X, equip Y, etc.)
- Spend points to unlock pacts

### Critical Math: Eye of Ayak F8

**Pact F8:** "Powered staff attack rate +3 ticks (min 1 tick), -8 max hit on 1H powered staves"

| | Without F8 | With F8 |
|---|-----------|---------|
| Max hit (full mage gear) | 33 | 25 |
| Attack speed | 1.8s (3-tick) | 0.6s (1-tick) |
| **DPS** | **9.17** | **20.83** |
| **Ratio** | — | **2.27x** |

**F8 is a no-brainer pick.** It transforms Eye of Ayak into a 1-tick weapon for ~2.3x boss DPS.

### Two Loadouts

**Bossing (Eye of Ayak):**
- F8 — 2.27x DPS via 1-tick speed
- Powered staff specifics, NOT a spellbook spell
- Tome of Water does NOT boost (built-in spell, not spellbook)

**Barrage Slayer (Kodai Wand):**
- F7 — Combat spell speed (probably stack 3 to hit 2-tick floor on Ice Barrage)
- L6 — Ice spells become water spells (unlocks I2 + Tome of Water synergy)
- I2 — +20% damage at 100% HP
- Tome of Water DOES boost (with L6)

### Pact Tier S Picks

1. **F8** — Eye of Ayak 1-tick (boss DPS)
2. **F7** — Combat spell speed ×3 stacks (barrage Slayer)
3. **L6** — Ice → water conversion
4. **I2** — Water spell +20% damage at full HP
5. **FA** — +1% mage damage per stack (take many)
6. **B1** — Magic level boost on rune regen
7. **C1-C4** — Rune-specific regen effects (prayer/healing/damage/defence)
