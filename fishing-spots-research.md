## VARLAMORE — Fishing Spots

### Real Fishing Spots (Yes, They Exist)

Varlamore has more fishing than it first appears. The 2024-2025 updates added spots across the mainland and Aldarin island.

#### 1. Lure/Bait Spots (Fly Fishing — Trout/Salmon/Pike)

These are standard river fly fishing spots identical to Barbarian Village or Shilo Village. They catch:
- **Raw trout** (20 Fishing, 50 XP) — Fly rod + feather
- **Raw salmon** (30 Fishing, 70 XP) — Fly rod + feather
- **Raw rainbow fish** (38 Fishing, 80 XP) — Fly rod + stripy feather
- **Raw pike** (25 Fishing, 60 XP) — Rod + bait

**Locations (all mainland Varlamore, no Sailing needed):**
- East of Nemus Retreat (Auburn Valley, fairy ring AIS nearby)
- South-East of Auburnvale (Auburn Valley)
- North of Ortus Farm (west of Civitas illa Fortis)
- North-East of Tal Teklan (Tlati Rainforest)
- South-East of the Farming Guild (Kebos border area — this may count as Kourend)

**This means you can fly fish for trout/salmon in Varlamore from day 1.**

#### 2. Net/Bait Spots (Shrimp/Anchovies/Sardine/Herring)

- **South-West of Kastori** (Tlati Rainforest, near Sulisal's shop)
- **Northern & North-Western coast of Aldarin** (island, accessible via charter ship or Sailing)
- Catches: Raw shrimps (1 Fishing), Raw anchovies (15), Raw sardine (5, bait), Raw herring (10, bait)

#### 3. Cage/Harpoon Spots (Lobster/Tuna/Swordfish)

- **Southern coast of Mistrock on Aldarin** (island)
- Catches: Raw lobster (40 Fishing, cage), Raw tuna (35, harpoon), Raw swordfish (50, harpoon)

#### 4. Big Net/Harpoon Spots (Mackerel/Cod/Bass/Shark)

- **Salvager Overlook** (north of Civitas illa Fortis)
- **Tal Teklan** (Tlati Rainforest — 2 spots)
- **Laguna Aurorae** (island, south-west of Aldarin — may need Sailing)
- Catches (Big Net): Raw mackerel (16), Raw cod (23), Raw bass (46), plus junk
- Catches (Harpoon): Raw shark (76 Fishing)

#### 5. Civitas illa Fortis Park Pond (Novelty)

- South of the Cothon harbour
- **Big net** fishing — NOT real fish
- Catches: House keys, caskets, jade, red topaz, broken glass, old boots
- Level 1 Fishing, ~7.5 XP per catch
- Spot never moves — extremely AFK
- Only useful for house keys (Stealing Valuables)

#### 6. Lantern Fishing (New — Requires Lit Lantern + Harpoon)

- **Laguna Aurorae** (island)
- Catches: Raw swordtip squid, Raw jumbo squid
- (Also available at Onyx Crest Buccaneers' Haven and Wintumber Island, but those are N/A for Leagues)

## KARAMJA — Fishing Spots

### 1. Musa Point — Net/Bait + Cage/Harpoon

**Location:** Behind Luthas' banana plantation on northern Karamja. The dock area.

**Net/Bait spots:**
- Raw shrimps (1 Fishing, 10 XP) — Small net
- Raw anchovies (15 Fishing, 40 XP) — Small net
- Raw sardine (5 Fishing, 20 XP) — Rod + bait
- Raw herring (10 Fishing, 30 XP) — Rod + bait

**Cage/Harpoon spots** (south of Fountain of Rune):
- Raw lobster (40 Fishing, 90 XP) — Lobster pot
- Raw tuna (35 Fishing, 80 XP) — Harpoon
- Raw swordfish (50 Fishing, 100 XP) — Harpoon

**Banking:** NO bank nearby. Very far from a bank. Deposit box at Port Sarim dock, but that requires paying 30gp for the boat. With Endless Harvest auto-banking, this is a non-issue in leagues.

**Equipment:** Buy everything from Picaria's or Sulisal's shop in Varlamore before sailing to Karamja, or use shops on Karamja.

### 2. Shilo Village — Fly Fishing (Trout/Salmon/Pike)

**Location:** River running through Shilo Village. 4 fishing spots.

**Quest requirement:** Shilo Village quest — **AUTO-COMPLETED when you unlock Karamja** in Demonic Pacts League. Jungle Potion (prerequisite) also auto-completes.

**Lure/Bait spots:**
- Raw trout (20 Fishing, 50 XP) — Fly rod + feather
- Raw salmon (30 Fishing, 70 XP) — Fly rod + feather
- Raw rainbow fish (38 Fishing, 80 XP) — Fly rod + stripy feather
- Raw pike (25 Fishing, 60 XP) — Rod + bait

**Banking:** Shilo Village has a bank. Very convenient. Agility shortcut (32 Agility) crosses the river to reach some spots faster.

**Shop:** Fernahei's Fishing Hut (in Shilo Village):
- Fishing rod: 5 stock, 5 gp
- Fly fishing rod: 5 stock, 5 gp
- Fishing bait: 200 stock, 3 gp
- Bait pack: 30 stock, 300 gp
- Feather: 800 stock, 2 gp
- Feather pack: 50 stock, 200 gp

### 3. Holy Lake / Karamja Lake — Karambwanji + Shrimp

**Location:** South of Tai Bwo Wannai, near fairy ring **CKR**.

**Net (karambwanji) spot:**
- Raw karambwanji (5 Fishing) — Small net
- Raw shrimps (1 Fishing) — also caught here
- Karambwanji are **stackable** — they don't fill your inventory
- Catch quantity scales with level: floor(Fishing/5) + 1 per catch
  - Level 5-9: 2 per catch
  - Level 50: 11 per catch
  - Level 99: 20 per catch

**IMPORTANT for karambwan bait:** This is where you get raw karambwanji to bait your karambwan vessel. You can also buy them from Tiadeche's shop (see below) for 10 gp each.

### 4. Karambwan Fishing — NE Karamja

**Location:** North of the Karamja Shipyard, east of harpie bug swarms. Fairy ring **DKP** is right next to the spot.

**Requirements:**
- 65 Fishing
- Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest completed (see quest section below)
- Karambwan vessel (loaded with raw karambwanji)

**Catches:**
- Raw karambwan (65 Fishing, 50 XP)

**Key properties:**
- **STATIC SPOT — never moves.** One of the most AFK fishing methods in the game.
- Fairy ring DKP is right next to it for banking (fairy ring to bank, load karambwanji, fairy ring back)

### 5. Brimhaven Fishing

**Location:** South of Brimhaven town.

The main Fishing wiki page mentions net/bait spots south of Brimhaven, but the official Fishing Spots page does NOT list Brimhaven as a separate location. The south-of-Brimhaven area overlaps with the Holy Lake / karambwanji fishing area.

**Practical summary:** Brimhaven itself is not a useful fishing destination. The nearby karambwanji spots (at Holy Lake / fairy ring CKR) are what matters.

### 6. Infernal Eels — Mor Ul Rek

**Location:** Inner city of Mor Ul Rek (TzHaar city), Karamja.

**Requirements:**
- 80 Fishing, 95 XP per catch
- **Oily fishing rod** (made by using blamish oil on a fishing rod — requires a harralander potion + blamish snail slime + fishing rod; no quest needed to make it, despite being associated with Heroes' Quest)
- Fishing bait
- **Ice gloves** (or Smiths gloves (i)) — must be EQUIPPED
- **Fire cape** — required to enter inner Mor Ul Rek

**Catches:**
- Infernal eel (80 Fishing, 95 XP)
- Smash with hammer for: Tokkul (29/35 chance), Onyx bolt tips (2/35 chance), Lava scale shards (4/35 chance)
- **NOT COOKABLE** — no Hotfoot synergy

## QUEST STATUS — Critical for Fishing Progression

### Auto-Completed Quests (Demonic Pacts League)

**When you unlock Karamja (automatic with first region pick):**
- **Shilo Village** — AUTO-COMPLETED (gives access to Shilo Village + fly fishing + Fernahei's shop)
- **Jungle Potion** — AUTO-COMPLETED (prerequisite for Shilo Village)

**NOT auto-completed:**
- **Tai Bwo Wannai Trio** — YOU MUST DO THIS MANUALLY
  - Requirements: 15 Agility, 30 Cooking, 5 Fishing, Jungle Potion completed (auto-completed)
  - Rewards: 5,000 Fishing XP, 5,000 Cooking XP, 2,500 Atk + 2,500 Str XP
  - Unlocks: Karambwan fishing + cooking, Tiadeche's Karambwan Stall, karambwan vessels
  - **Must complete to fish karambwan at DKP**

**When you unlock Desert (2nd pick):**
- Many quests auto-complete (DT1, DT2, Tourist Trap, Waterfall, etc.)
- Tempoross accessible at Ruins of Unkah (35 Fishing)

**When you unlock Kandarin (3rd pick):**
- **Swan Song** — AUTO-COMPLETED (gives access to Piscatoris for monkfish)
- King's Ransom, Monkey Madness II, Dragon Slayer II auto-complete

**When you unlock Kourend (1st pick):**
- A Kingdom Divided + all prerequisites auto-complete
- Anglerfish favour requirement removed (Jan 2024) — no favour needed
- Barbarian Training (for barbarian fishing) — NOT a quest, just talk to Otto. Available in Kandarin (not Kourend).

### Karambwanji — Where to Get Bait

**Option 1 (Free, no quest):** Catch with small fishing net at Holy Lake, south of Tai Bwo Wannai, fairy ring CKR. Level 5 Fishing. Stackable, 2-20 per catch based on level.

**Option 2 (Buy, requires quest):** Buy from Tiadeche's Karambwan Stall in Tai Bwo Wannai for 10 gp each, 50 stock. Requires Tai Bwo Wannai Trio completion. **Infinite stock in leagues.**

**No other source.** They are untradeable. You must net-fish them or buy from Tiadeche.

### Karambwan Vessels — Where to Get

- **During quest:** Lubufu (south of Brimhaven) gives you one.
- **After quest:** Buy from Tiadeche's Karambwan Stall for 2 gp each, 2 stock.
- Vessels are loaded with raw karambwanji to become "karambwan vessel (baited)."

## EARLY-GAME FISHING PROGRESSION

### Phase 0: Day 1 in Varlamore (Levels 1-20+)

**Immediately available:**
1. Buy fly fishing rod + feathers from **Picaria's Fishing Shop** (Sunset Coast) or **Sulisal's** (Kastori)
2. Also buy fishing rod + bait, small net, harpoon, lobster pot — stock up on everything
3. **Net fish shrimps** at Kastori (SW of town) or Aldarin coast for levels 1-15 (~10 XP each)
4. **Bait fish sardine** (5+) and herring (10+) at same spots (~20/30 XP each)
5. At **level 20**, move to a Varlamore fly fishing spot:
   - North of Ortus Farm (closest to Civitas illa Fortis)
   - East of Nemus Retreat (Auburn Valley, fairy ring AIS)
   - North-East of Tal Teklan
6. Fly fish **trout** (20+, 50 XP) and **salmon** (30+, 70 XP) with Hotfoot auto-cooking

**You do NOT need to leave Varlamore for early fishing.** Trout/salmon are available on the Varlamore mainland.

### Phase 1: Karamja Opens (auto-unlock with first region pick)

**Auto-completed: Shilo Village + Jungle Potion**

7. Travel to **Shilo Village** for better fly fishing:
   - 4 spots, bank nearby, Fernahei's shop for more feathers
   - Continue trout/salmon until 35+ Fishing
8. At level 35+, optionally fish **tuna** at Musa Point cage/harpoon spots (80 XP, harpoon)
9. At level 40, fish **lobster** at Musa Point (90 XP, lobster pot)

**Quest: Tai Bwo Wannai Trio** (do this ASAP for karambwan access):
- Requirements: 15 Agility, 30 Cooking, 5 Fishing (trivial with league XP)
- Jungle Potion prerequisite is auto-completed
- Rewards: 5k Fishing XP, 5k Cooking XP
- Unlocks karambwan fishing + Tiadeche's shop

10. After TWW Trio, catch some **karambwanji** at Holy Lake (fairy ring CKR) or buy from Tiadeche (10 gp each)
11. At level **65 Fishing**, start **karambwan** at fairy ring DKP
    - Static spot, ultra-AFK, 50 XP/catch, 190 Cooking XP auto-cooked via Hotfoot

### Phase 2: Kourend Opens (1st region pick, ~90 tasks)

12. **Anglerfish** become available at 82 Fishing (Port Piscarilius)
    - Buy sandworms from Tynan's (90 gp each, infinite)
    - 120 Fishing XP, 230 Cooking XP per catch — best in your regions
13. **Hosidius fly fishing** available (east of kitchen, bank chest nearby)
14. **Aerial fishing** at Lake Molch (43 Fishing + 35 Hunter) for combined XP

### Phase 3: Desert Opens (2nd region pick, ~200 tasks)

15. **Tempoross** at Ruins of Unkah (35 Fishing) — do for rewards
    - Dragon harpoon (faster harpooning everywhere)
    - **Tome of Water** (mage BIS off-hand with L6 pact)
    - Spirit flakes, Fish barrel
16. **Cage/Harpoon at Ruins of Unkah** — 3 spots near bank, no desert heat

### Phase 4: Kandarin Opens (3rd region pick, ~400 tasks)

17. **Barbarian fishing** at Otto's Grotto — best raw Fishing XP/hr
18. **Monkfish** at Piscatoris (Swan Song auto-completed) — 120 XP, no bait, high catch rate
19. **Sharks** at Catherby/Fishing Guild — 76 Fishing, harpoon, no bait
20. **Fishing Trawler** at Port Khazard — Angler's outfit (2.5% bonus)
21. **Fishing Guild** — all spot types, invisible +7 boost, closest bank
