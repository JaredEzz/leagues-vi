## 3rd Region Analysis

### Option A: Kandarin (Recommended)

**Gets you:**
| Item | What | Why It Matters |
|------|------|---------------|
| **Necklace of Anguish** | +15 ranged atk, +5 ranged str | BIS neck. Massive DPS increase. Nothing else comes close |
| **Chinchompas** | MM2 tunnels | Best Ranged training method in the game |
| **Demonic Gorillas** | Zenyte drops + Ballista pieces | Farm gorillas for zenytes |
| **Kraken** | Trident (swap weapon for mage tasks) | Decent Slayer boss |
| **Smoke Devils** | Slayer at 93 | Good task |
| **Lunar Spells** | Utility | Vengeance, NPC Contact for Slayer |

**What you miss:** Ava's Assembler (cape slot), Archers Ring

**Cape workaround:** Ranged Skillcape (99 Ranged) from ranged master, or Accumulator if available through other means. Not as good as Assembler but functional.

### Option B: Fremennik

**Gets you:**

| **Basilisk Knights** | Basilisk Jaw (Neitiznot Faceguard) | Melee helm, less relevant for ranged |

**What you miss:** Necklace of Anguish (+15 ranged atk, +5 ranged str), chinchompa training

### Option C: Tirannwn

**Gets you:**
| Item | What | Why It Matters |

### Option D: Asgarnia

**Gets you:**
| Item | What | Why It Matters |
|------|------|---------------|
| **Armadyl Armour** | Ranged armour | Worse than Masori (which you have from ToA). Redundant |

**Verdict:** Mostly redundant. Masori > Armadyl, TBow > ACB in most content. Pegasian boots are nice but not worth a region slot.

## Verdict

### Ranking

| Rank | Region | Key Get | Key Miss |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| **1** | **Kandarin** | Anguish (+15 atk, +5 str), chinchompa training, Lunar spells | Assembler, Archers Ring |
| **2** | **Fremennik** | Assembler (BIS cape), Archers Ring, Vorkath practice | Anguish, chinchompa training |
| **3** | **Tirannwn** | BowFa + Crystal set, Blowpipe, CG practice | Anguish, Assembler, Archers Ring |
| **4** | Asgarnia | Pegasian boots | Almost everything (Masori > Armadyl) |

### Why Kandarin Wins

The Necklace of Anguish is **+15 ranged attack and +5 ranged strength** in a single slot. No other necklace comes close for ranged. Compare:

| Necklace | Ranged Atk | Ranged Str |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| Anguish | +15 | +5 |
| Fury | +10 | 0 |
| Glory | +6 | 0 |

That +5 ranged strength is a direct max hit increase. It's the single biggest DPS upgrade available from a 3rd region.

Assembler (Fremennik) is +8 ranged atk and +2 ranged str — strong, but smaller impact than Anguish. You can somewhat compensate with Ranged Skillcape or other capes. There's no substitute for Anguish.

Chinchompa training access (MM2 tunnels, Kandarin) is also huge — it's by far the fastest Ranged XP method, important for a maxing goal.

## Alternative: If You Want Boss Practice Over DPS

Pick **Fremennik** instead of Kandarin if:
- You want Vorkath as a ranged practice boss
- You value Assembler's ammo conservation for ironman sustainability
- You want Archers Ring for accuracy stacking

Pick **Tirannwn** instead if:
- You want Corrupted Gauntlet as hard content
- BowFa + Crystal set for ammo-free ranged (huge ironman QoL)
- Blowpipe for fast DPS content
- You're OK missing both Anguish AND Assembler
