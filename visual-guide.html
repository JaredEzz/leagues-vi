Mage Build — BIS Gear

Eye of Ayak

Varlamore — Day 1

1H powered staff, 3-tick (fastest). Built-in spell, drains target magic defence on spec. Core mage weapon.

Source: Doom of Mokhaiotl (delve 3+)

Confliction Gauntlets

Kandarin (Phase 4)

+20 mage atk, +7% mage dmg. Passive re-rolls accuracy on miss. Only works with 1H weapons — perfect with Eye of Ayak.

Zenyte (Ka) + Mokhaiotl Cloth (V) + 10k demon tears + 73 Crafting real

Kodai Wand

CoX + MTA

+28 mage atk, +15% mage dmg. Autocasts Ancients. Unlimited water runes. 15% chance to negate rune cost. Barrage Slayer swap.

Dragon Hunter Wand

Varlamore — Day 1

+16 mage atk, +10% mage dmg. **+75% accuracy, +40% damage vs dragons.** 1H, autocasts Ancients. Stacks with Slayer helm. From Hueycoatl (1/105). HUGE for CoX (Vasa, Tekton-adjacent dragons) and Hydra Slayer.

Source: Hueycoatl, Varlamore

Ancestral Set

Chambers of Xeric

+69 mage atk, +9% mage dmg total. BIS mage armor. Wear the hat instead of Slayer helm (Culling Spree gives helm effect free).

Avernic Treads

Varlamore — Day 1

+11 mage atk, +1% mage dmg. Hybrid boots. From Doom of Mokhaiotl delve 4+. Eternal upgrade locked (Cerberus/Asgarnia).

Occult Necklace

Kandarin (93 Slayer)

+12 mage atk, +5% mage dmg. Only necklace with magic damage %. Drops from Thermonuclear Smoke Devil.

Devil's Element

Echo Thermy (Kandarin)

+20 mage atk, +6% mage dmg. Doubles NPC elemental weaknesses. Unlimited elemental runes. Potential BIS offhand.

Tumeken's Shadow

ToA (Desert)

2H raw DPS staff. No Confliction passive. Use for content where raw damage beats accuracy.
