# Progression — Phase-by-Phase Options

*Broad phases defined by region unlocks and relic tiers. Pick what appeals to you at each phase — these are options, not a strict order. Specific day-1 route handled by Doubleshine or similar.*

*Point thresholds based on Raging Echoes (likely similar). Task counts for region unlocks may differ — update when confirmed.*

---

## Phase 1: Varlamore + Karamja

**Relics:** T1 Abundance (start) → working toward T2 (750 pts)
**Regions:** Varlamore (start), Karamja (auto-unlocks at 1st region pick)
**Goal:** Complete ~90 tasks for 1st region unlock

### Skills Available

| Thieving | Bazaar pickpocketing | Street Urchin = easy |

| Herblore | Early potions from farmed herbs | Limited until Mixology at 50 real |
| Cooking | Cook whatever you catch/kill | |

### What Carries You Forward

- Varlamore has 7 bosses + tons of skills = lots of easy/medium/hard tasks
- Karamja adds more tasks once it auto-unlocks
- Mining + Agility + Hunter + Thieving + RC all generate task completions fast
- Boss KC tasks (kill Doom once, kill Vardorvis once, etc.) are quick points

### Echo Bosses Available

| Echo Boss | Normal Version | Echo Drop | Drop Rate | Strategy |
|-----------|---------------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Echo Amoxliatl** | Amoxliatl (Varlamore) | Four-pronged weapon (TBD) | TBD | **Do early.** Normal Amoxliatl is easy enough to farm echo orbs. Gear isn't too rare |

Other Varlamore bosses (Moons of Peril, Hueycoatl, etc.) may have echo variants revealed later. **Do normal versions early** — the drops are good for your account and not too rare. Save hard-grind boss farming for T4 (5x drop rate).

### Decision Point: Doom of Mokhaiotl Early?

**Pro:** Eye of Ayak early = your mage weapon for the rest of the league. Avernic Treads + Mokhaiotl Cloth too.
**Con:** Drop rates are worse at low delve levels (1/2,000 at delve 3). No 5x drop rate yet (that's T4). Gear is weak.
**Middle ground:** Attempt some kills for the boss KC tasks (points), but don't hard-grind for drops yet. Come back at T4 (5x drops).

## Phase 2: + First Region (Kourend recommended)

**Relics:** T2 (750 pts) → T3 Evil Eye (1,500 pts) → working toward T4 (2,500 pts)
**Regions:** Varlamore, Karamja, **Kourend**
**Goal:** Complete ~200 tasks for 2nd region unlock

### T2 Relic Pick: Hotfoot vs Woodsman

Pick now. See [relics.md](relics.md) for full analysis. Leaning Hotfoot (solves Agility, the worst grind).

### New Skills Unlocked

| Skill | Method | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Firemaking | **Wintertodt** | HP doesn't matter (warmth meter). Need a rune axe. Do whenever, bank carts, open later with high Farming (67+) / Herblore (56+) for best seeds/herbs. If Woodsman: skip WT but miss supply rewards |
| Woodcutting | Redwoods (80 real with Abundance) | AFK to 99. If Woodsman: also trains FM |
| Smithing | Anvil smithing (Kourend furnaces) | BF is Fremennik (locked). Giants' Foundry comes with Desert (Phase 3) |
| Farming | **Farming Guild contracts** | Seed pipeline starts. Hespori. Master Farmer. This compounds over time — start ASAP |
| Runecraft | Blood runes (67 real) / Soul runes (80 real) | AFK endgame RC |
| Slayer | **Catacombs of Kourend** | Multi-combat melee Slayer. No barrage yet (waiting for Desert + Culling Spree) |
| Firemaking (alt) | Forestry auto-burn (if Woodsman) | Passive through WC |
| Fletching (alt) | Batch processing (if Woodsman) | Near-instant |

### New Bosses

| Boss | Drops Worth Targeting | Notes |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| **Chambers of Xeric** | Ancestral (hat/top/bottom), Kodai Insignia, Augury prayer, DHCB | Core mage gear. Ancestral = +9% magic dmg. Kodai = barrage weapon. Augury = BIS mage prayer |
| **Alchemical Hydra** | Hydra leather, Hydra's eye/fang/heart | Req 95 Slayer. Endgame boss — not yet |
| **Sarachnis** | Cudgel, Giant egg sac | Easy mid-game boss |

| **Wintertodt** | Seeds, herbs, ores, logs, Dragon Axe (1/10k), Tome of Fire (1/1k), Phoenix pet (1/5k) | Rewards scale with skills. Bank carts, open at Farming 67+/Herblore 56+ real. Rates improve massively at T4 (5x drops). Do whenever you have a rune axe |
| **Hespori** | Tree seeds | Farming Guild. Plant Hespori seed → kill → seeds |

### What Carries You Forward

- Kourend has massive task density (Wintertodt tasks, Slayer tasks, CoX tasks, Farming tasks, Skotizo, Tithe Farm, etc.)
- Farming contracts generate passive tasks (complete X contracts)
- Slayer tasks in Catacombs = combat tasks + Slayer tasks simultaneously
- CoX completions = boss tasks
- **T3 Evil Eye (1,500 pts):** Boss teleports. Instant travel to CoX, Doom of Mokhaiotl, every boss. Huge efficiency gain.

### Echo Bosses Available

| Echo Boss | Echo Drop | Drop Rate | Strategy |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Echo Amoxliatl** | Four-pronged weapon (TBD) | TBD | Continue from Phase 1 |
| **Echo Hespori** | Nature's Reprisal (3-style weapon) | 1/15, guaranteed at 30 KC | Easy farm. Kill Hespori whenever it grows. Nature's Reprisal is a fun multi-style weapon |

### Bossing Options

At this phase you have Varlamore bosses + CoX. With Abundance +10 and some combat training:

| Content | When Ready | What You Get |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| CoX solos/duos | Once combat stats are decent (~70s-80s) | Ancestral, Kodai insignia, Augury. Start learning |
| Doom of Mokhaiotl | Ongoing from Phase 1 | Eye of Ayak, Avernic Treads. Better with higher delve levels |
| Fight Caves | Early (easy with leagues buffs) | Fire Cape. Quick task completion |
| Hespori + Echo Hespori | Every Hespori seed | Tree seeds + Nature's Reprisal (echo). Farming Guild |

## Phase 3: + Second Region (Desert recommended)

**Relics:** T4 Conniving Clues (2,500 pts) → T5 Nature's Accord (5,000 pts) → working toward T6 (8,000 pts)
**Regions:** Varlamore, Karamja, Kourend, **Desert**
**Goal:** Complete ~400 tasks for 3rd region unlock

### What Turns On

| Unlock | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Ancient Magicks** | Ice Burst/Barrage available. Can use in Catacombs once you have Culling Spree (T6). Until then, use for general combat |
| **T4: 5x drop rates** | Doom of Mokhaiotl farming gets MUCH better. Eye of Ayak, Ancestral from CoX, all boss drops |
| **T4: 8x minigame points** | Fishing Trawler, Tempoross, Tithe Farm, Wintertodt all faster |
| **T4: Conniving Clues** | Clues from creatures at 1/15. Min steps, max rewards. Casket chaining |
| **T5: Nature's Accord** | Farming explodes. 10x noted yield. Plants never die. Fairy mushroom teleport. **Herblore supply solved** |

### New Skills Unlocked

| Skill | Method | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Magic | **Ancient Magicks** for combat. Save full barrage Slayer for Culling Spree (T6) | Can still burst/barrage outside Catacombs or use for general bossing |
| Crafting | **Al Kharid gem trader** + Abundance GP | Push to 83 for Confliction Gauntlets |
| Smithing | **Giants' Foundry** (Giants' Plateau) | Finally unlocked! ~4.4M/hr at 16x with rune bars. Feed it bars + recycled equipment |
| Thieving | Blackjacking (Pollnivneach) | Faster than Ardy Knights if you want |
| Herblore | Nature's Accord 10x herb yield = unlimited supply → **Mixology** | Supply bottleneck gone |
| Farming | Nature's Accord + Farming contracts = **trivially fast** | 10x yield, plants never die, 20% seed save |

### New Bosses

| Boss | Drops Worth Targeting | Notes |
|------|----------------------|-------|
| **Tombs of Amascut** | Tumeken's Shadow, Masori armour, Elidinis' Ward, Lightbearer | Scalable difficulty (invocations). Evil Eye teleports you there. Shadow = 2H mage swap. Ward = offhand |
| **Kalphite Queen** | KQ head (diary), Dragon chain | Task boss |
| **Tempoross** | Spirit Angler recolor, Spirit Flakes, Tiny Tempor pet | Fishing minigame boss. 8x minigame points |
| **Echo Amoxliatl** | Echo rewards (TBD) | Harder variant, better drops |

### What Carries You Forward

- **ToA is a points machine** — completions at various invocation levels = tons of tasks
- **Clue engine starts:** Conniving Clues + Slayer creature kills (1/15 clue rate) = drowning in clues. Each completion = points
- Desert + Kourend tasks combined = massive task pool
- **5x drop rates** make boss farming actually rewarding now
- Nature's Accord Farming tasks (harvest X, grow X) complete instantly with 10x yield + 1-min ticks

### Bossing Options

| Content | When Ready | What You Get |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **ToA (entry mode → normal)** | Once you have decent mage/range gear | Shadow, Ward, Masori. Scale with invocations |
| **CoX** (continued) | Ongoing | Ancestral, Kodai, Augury |
| **Doom of Mokhaiotl** | Now with 5x drops | Eye of Ayak, Avernic Treads, Mokhaiotl Cloth. Much better rates |
| Inferno attempts | If you're feeling it | Infernal Cape. Hard but Flask of Fervour is still 2 tiers away |

### Echo Boss Strategy

**Do early (before T4 5x drops):** Normal Amoxliatl, Moons of Peril, Hespori — drops are useful and not too rare. Farm echo orbs from normal versions, attempt echo variants for bonus drops.

**Wait for T4 5x drops (2,500 pts):** Doom of Mokhaiotl (Eye of Ayak, Avernic Treads), CoX (Ancestral), ToA (Shadow, Ward). Rare drops benefit massively from 5x multiplier.

**Wait for Kandarin (Phase 4):** Echo Thermonuclear Smoke Devil → Devil's element (potential BIS offhand, see below).

### Key Gear Milestone

By mid-Phase 3 you could have:
- Eye of Ayak + Avernic Treads (Doom of Mokhaiotl, especially with 5x drops)
- Ancestral pieces (CoX)
- Kodai Wand (MTA Master Wand + CoX insignia)
- Augury (CoX)
- Elidinis' Ward base (ToA)
- Ancient Magicks (Desert quest)

**Missing:** Confliction Gauntlets (need zenyte from Kandarin), Occult Necklace (need 93 Slayer + Kandarin), **Devil's element** (Echo Thermy, Kandarin)

## Phase 4: + Third Region (Kandarin) + Culling Spree

**Relics:** T6 Culling Spree (8,000 pts) → T7 16x multiplier (16,000 pts) → T8 Flask (25,000 pts)
**Regions:** All five — Varlamore, Karamja, Kourend, Desert, **Kandarin**

### What Turns On

| Unlock | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **T6: Culling Spree** | Barrage Slayer fully online. Free Slayer helm → Ancestral Hat. Choose tasks. Superior chains → elite clues |
| **Kandarin content** | Zenytes, Occult, chins, fishing, Kraken, Lunar spells |
| **T7: 16x multiplier** | Max XP rate. Mop up remaining 99s fast |

### Mage Build Completion

| Item | Source | Status |
|------|--------|--------|
| Eye of Ayak | Doom of Mokhaiotl | Should have by now (5x drops since Phase 3) |
| Confliction Gauntlets | Zenyte (Demonic Gorillas, Ka) + Mokhaiotl Cloth (V) + 10k demon tears | **Farm zenytes NOW** |
| Ancestral set | CoX (Ko) | Should have pieces by now |
| Kodai Wand | Master Wand (MTA, D) + Insignia (CoX, Ko) | Should have by now |
| Occult Necklace | Thermonuclear Smoke Devil (Ka) | **Need 93 Slayer. Use Culling Spree to pick Smoke Devil tasks** |
| Avernic Treads | Doom of Mokhaiotl | Should have by now |
| Elidinis' Ward | ToA (D) | Should have by now |
| Augury | CoX (Ko) | Should have by now |

### Barrage Slayer Engine (Culling Spree Active)

Full setup now available:
- Kodai Wand + Ancestral (head free — no Slayer helm needed) + Confliction + Occult + Avernic Treads + Ward
- Choose Dust Devils / Nechryaels / Smoke Devils every task
- Superior chains → elite clues → Conniving Clues max rewards → casket chains → points

**This is your main activity from T6 to T8.** Trains Magic, Defence, Slayer, and generates massive league points.

## Phase 5: Endgame (T8: Flask of Fervour)

**25,000+ points**

### Flask of Fervour Unlocked

Full HP + Prayer + Spec restore on ~1-2 min cooldown. 2.4s invulnerability. Push everything:

| Content | Goal |
|---------|------|
| **ToA high invocations** | Push difficulty. Shadow farming. League points from invocation milestones |
| **Inferno** | Infernal Cape. Flask makes learning much more forgiving |
| **CoX challenge mode** | Harder CoX. Better loot. Points |
| **Echo Bosses** | Harder variants of all bosses. Echo Amoxliatl, Echo Thermy, Echo Hespori |
| **Sol Heredit** | Colosseum. Very hard. Flask helps survive |
| **Remaining 99s** | Mop up anything left at 16x |

### Points Target

| Reward Tier | Points | Notes |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| Dragon | ~15,000-20,000 | Minimum for all must-have rewards |
| Rune+ | ~25,000+ | All nice-to-haves too |

You should comfortably hit Dragon+ from barrage Slayer + clue chains + boss tasks + skill completions.
