# Boss Teleport Guide -- Every Boss in 5 Regions (Without Evil Eye)

*Comprehensive boss list for Varlamore + Karamja + Kourend/Kebos + Desert + Kandarin.*
*Assumes: Fairy rings (Nature's Accord mushroom), Map of Alacrity, 99 Magic, Ancient/Arceuus spellbooks, Xeric's talisman, Digsite pendant, Magic cape (5 swaps/day), Quest cape, all diary rewards, standard jewelry teleports (glory, combat bracelet, games necklace, ring of wealth, ring of dueling, skills necklace, etc.), standard teleport tablets.*

**Evil Eye (T3 relic)** teleports you to the entrance of any boss on the Combat Achievements list. This document shows the BEST alternative when you do NOT have Evil Eye (i.e., you picked Map of Alacrity at T3 instead).

---

## VARLAMORE

### 1. Doom of Mokhaiotl
- **Location:** Ruins of Mokhaiotl, deep beneath Tlati Rainforest
- **Requirements:** The Final Dawn quest
- **Drops:** Eye of Ayak, Avernic Treads, Mokhaiotl Cloth
- **Best teleport:** Pendant of Ates -> Kastori waystone, run NW to Tonali Cavern entrance. Alternatively, Mokhaiotl Waystone (dropped by boss, direct teleport once obtained). Quetzal Whistle to Tal Teklan, run east also works.
- **Travel time:** ~60-90s from Pendant of Ates (first time); ~15-20s with Mokhaiotl Waystone
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~45-75s per trip before waystone; ~0-15s after waystone (waystone is nearly equivalent)

### 2. Sol Heredit (Fortis Colosseum)
- **Location:** Fortis Colosseum, cliffs east of Civitas illa Fortis, Varlamore
- **Requirements:** None (12-wave gauntlet, Sol Heredit is wave 12)
- **Drops:** Dizana's Quiver, Sunfire Fanatic armour, Echo Boots, Tonalztics of Ralos
- **Best teleport:** Ring of Dueling -> Fortis Colosseum (unlocked at 12,000 glory). Alternatively, fairy ring AIS to Auburn Valley then run NE, or Quetzal Transport to Civitas illa Fortis.
- **Travel time:** ~5-10s with Ring of Dueling (direct teleport); ~60-90s from fairy ring AIS
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0-5s (Ring of Dueling is already nearly instant)

### 3. The Hueycoatl
- **Location:** The Darkfrost, Varlamore
- **Requirements:** None (group boss, up to 20 players)
- **Drops:** Dragon Hunter Wand (1/105), Hueycoatl Hide, Sunfire Splinters
- **Best teleport:** Pendant of Ates -> Darkfrost (after activating Statue of Ates near base camp). Quetzal Transport to Quetzacalli Gorge also works.
- **Travel time:** ~10-20s from Pendant of Ates
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~5-15s

### 4. Amoxliatl
- **Location:** Ruins of Tapoyauik, western section, Varlamore
- **Requirements:** The Heart of Darkness quest
- **Drops:** Tonalztics of Ralos, Glacial Temotli, Pendant of Ates
- **Best teleport:** Pendant of Ates -> Twilight Temple (near the lift). Lift Platform goes directly to mid-level of Tapoyauik ruins.
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s from Pendant of Ates + lift
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 5. Echo Amoxliatl
- **Location:** Same as Amoxliatl (Ruins of Tapoyauik), requires Varlamore echo orb
- **Requirements:** Echo orb from Amoxliatl kills
- **Drops:** Twin weapon (TBD enhanced stats)
- **Best teleport:** Same as Amoxliatl above
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 6. Moons of Peril
- **Location:** Neypotzli, prison beneath Cam Torum, Varlamore
- **Requirements:** Perilous Moons quest
- **Drops:** Eclipse Atlatl, Dual Macuahuitl, Blue Moon Spear, Eclipse Moon set, Blood Moon set
- **Best teleport:** Calcified Moth teleport to Cam Torum, then run north to Neypotzli entrance. Pendant of Ates -> Ralos' Rise then Agility shortcut (47 Agility) down also works. Map of Alacrity -> Cam Torum agility shortcut could be fastest.
- **Travel time:** ~30-45s
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~20-35s

### 7. Vardorvis
- **Location:** The Stranglewood Ritual Site (Stranglewood is part of Varlamore, borders Kebos)
- **Requirements:** Desert Treasure II quest
- **Drops:** Soulreaper Axe, Ultor Ring, Virtus robes, Awakener's Orb
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring BLS (south of Mount Quidamortem) -> rowboat to Stranglewood -> run NW to Ritual Site. Or Xeric's Honour (talisman) -> rowboat. Ring of Shadows -> Stranglewood (requires strangled tablet drop from Vardorvis, 1/25).
- **Travel time:** ~60-90s from BLS/Xeric's Honour; ~10-15s with Ring of Shadows (once tablet obtained)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~50-80s before Ring of Shadows; ~0-10s after

### 8. Gemstone Crab
- **Location:** Roaming between 3 mines in Tlati Rainforest, Varlamore
- **Requirements:** Children of the Sun quest (Varlamore access)
- **Drops:** Gems (sapphire through dragonstone)
- **Best teleport:** Pendant of Ates -> Kastori, then find nearest cave entrance. Fairy ring AIS to Auburn Valley, then Achilka's boat also works.
- **Travel time:** ~30-60s (depends on current spawn location)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~20-50s

## KARAMJA

### 9. TzTok-Jad (Fight Caves)
- **Location:** TzHaar Fight Cave, NW Mor Ul Rek (outer city), beneath Karamja Volcano
- **Requirements:** None (63 waves then Jad)
- **Drops:** Fire Cape, 8,032 Tokkul
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring BLP (direct to Mor Ul Rek), run north to Fight Caves entrance. Alternative: Minigame teleport -> TzHaar Fight Pit, then walk to caves.
- **Travel time:** ~15-20s from fairy ring BLP
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-15s

### 10. TzKal-Zuk (Inferno)
- **Location:** Inner Mor Ul Rek, beneath Karamja Volcano
- **Requirements:** Must sacrifice a Fire Cape to TzHaar-Ket-Keh for first-time access to inner city
- **Drops:** Infernal Cape, 16,440 Tokkul
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring BLP -> run to inner city entrance (need Fire Cape to enter inner area) -> run to Inferno entrance. 
- **Travel time:** ~25-35s from BLP
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~15-25s

### 11. TzHaar-Ket-Rak's Challenges
- **Location:** Inner Mor Ul Rek, south of bank
- **Requirements:** Fire Cape for inner city access; challenges 3-6 require Inferno completion
- **Drops:** Tokkul
- **Best teleport:** Same as Inferno -- fairy ring BLP -> inner city
- **Travel time:** ~25-35s from BLP
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~15-25s

## KOUREND & KEBOS

### 12. Chambers of Xeric (CoX)
- **Location:** Beneath Mount Quidamortem, Kebos Lowlands
- **Requirements:** None (but high combat stats needed)
- **Drops:** Ancestral (hat/top/bottom), Kodai Insignia, Augury scroll, DHCB, Twisted Bow, Dragon Claws, Dexterous Prayer Scroll
- **Best teleport:** Xeric's talisman -> Xeric's Honour (requires ancient tablet unlock from CoX). Fairy ring BLS (south of Quidamortem, run north). Lovakengj Minecart to Quidamortem (free with The Forsaken Tower).
- **Travel time:** ~10-15s from Xeric's Honour; ~30-45s from fairy ring BLS; ~20-30s from minecart
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0-10s from Xeric's Honour (already very fast)

### 13. Chambers of Xeric: Challenge Mode
- **Location:** Same as CoX
- **Requirements:** Same + higher stats
- **Best teleport:** Same as CoX
- **Travel time:** Same as CoX
- **Evil Eye saves:** Same as CoX

### 14. Alchemical Hydra
- **Location:** Lower level of Karuulm Slayer Dungeon, Mount Karuulm
- **Requirements:** 95 Slayer, on a hydra task
- **Drops:** Hydra leather, Hydra's eye/fang/heart (Brimstone Ring components), Dragon Hunter Lance
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring CIR (south of Mount Karuulm) -> enter dungeon -> navigate to lower level (88 Agility shortcut via mysterious pipe speeds this up significantly). Rada's Blessing 3/4 teleport to Karuulm also works. Skills necklace -> Farming Guild, run NE.
- **Travel time:** ~45-60s from CIR without pipe shortcut; ~30-40s with 88 Agility shortcut; Map of Alacrity to the pipe shortcut could cut this further
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~25-50s (significant -- Hydra is deep in a dungeon)

### 15. Sarachnis
- **Location:** Burial tomb, southern Forthos Dungeon, near Hosidius
- **Requirements:** Slash weapon/knife to cut webs
- **Drops:** Sarachnis Cudgel, Giant Egg Sac
- **Best teleport:** Xeric's talisman -> Xeric's Glade (Hosidius) -> run south to Forthos Ruin entrance. Spirit tree to Hosidius (if Nature's Accord mushroom nearby). Kharedst's memoirs -> "Lunch by the Lancalliums" (River Hos, NE of ruin).
- **Travel time:** ~40-60s from Xeric's Glade (includes dungeon navigation)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~30-50s

### 16. Skotizo
- **Location:** Beneath Catacombs of Kourend (accessed via Dark Totem on altar)
- **Requirements:** Dark Totem (assembled from 3 pieces dropped in Catacombs)
- **Drops:** Uncut Onyx, Dark Claw, Shard of Darkness, Jar of Darkness
- **Best teleport:** Xeric's talisman -> Xeric's Heart (Kourend Castle courtyard) -> enter Catacombs statue -> altar in center. Arceuus spellbook Kourend Castle Teleport also works.
- **Travel time:** ~20-30s from Xeric's Heart to altar
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 17. Yama
- **Location:** Yama's Domain, depths of Chasm of Fire, Kourend (Shayzien area)
- **Requirements:** A Kingdom Divided quest
- **Drops:** Oathplate armour, Soulflame Horn
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring DJR (right next to Chasm of Fire entrance) -> descend to bottom floor -> speak to Voice of Yama -> enter domain. Chasm teleport scroll (consumable, goes directly to bottom next to Voice of Yama).
- **Travel time:** ~20-30s from fairy ring DJR; ~5-10s with chasm teleport scroll
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~15-25s from DJR; ~0-5s with scroll

### 18. Hespori
- **Location:** Cave in west wing of Farming Guild, Kourend
- **Requirements:** 65 Farming (boostable), Hespori seed planted and fully grown
- **Drops:** Tree seeds, White Lily seed
- **Best teleport:** Skills necklace -> Farming Guild (teleports inside with 45+ Farming) -> run to Hespori cave in west wing. Fairy ring CIR (south of Karuulm) -> run SW to guild.
- **Travel time:** ~10-15s from Skills necklace
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0-10s (Skills necklace is very close)

### 19. Echo Hespori
- **Location:** Same as Hespori (Farming Guild cave), requires Kourend echo orb
- **Requirements:** Echo orb from Hespori kills
- **Drops:** Nature's Reprisal shortbow (1/15, guaranteed at 30 KC)
- **Best teleport:** Same as Hespori
- **Travel time:** ~10-15s
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0-10s

### 20. Wintertodt
- **Location:** Northern Tundras, north of Arceuus, Great Kourend
- **Requirements:** None (but bring warm items)
- **Drops:** Dragon Axe (1/10k), Tome of Fire (1/1k), Phoenix pet, supply crates
- **Best teleport:** Games necklace -> Wintertodt Camp (direct teleport, very fast). Fairy ring CIS -> run west. Lovakengj Minecart to Northern Tundras.
- **Travel time:** ~5s from Games necklace (basically instant)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0s (Games necklace is equivalent or faster)

### 21. Lizardman Shaman
- **Location:** Multiple: Lizardman Canyon (Kebos), Lizardman Caves (Shayzien), Lizardman Temple (beneath Molch)
- **Requirements:** None (but Slayer task required for Lizardman Caves location)
- **Drops:** Dragon Warhammer (1/3,000)
- **Best teleport:** For Molch Temple: Skills necklace -> Farming Guild, run south across bridge. For Lizardman Caves: Xeric's talisman -> Xeric's Lookout (Shayzien), run west. Fairy ring DJR -> run SW to Molch.
- **Travel time:** ~30-45s depending on location
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~20-35s (not on CA boss list, Evil Eye may not work here -- Shamans are NOT a CA boss, only a notable monster)

### 22. The Mimic
- **Location:** Watson's House, Hosidius (upstairs, present mimic casket to strange casket)
- **Requirements:** Elite or Master clue mimic casket (1/35 elite, 1/15 master)
- **Drops:** 3rd Age items (from clue loot table)
- **Best teleport:** Watson Teleport (from quest). Xeric's talisman -> Xeric's Glade (Hosidius) -> run north to Watson's. Spirit tree to Hosidius area.
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s from Xeric's Glade
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

## DESERT (KHARIDIAN DESERT)

### 23. Tombs of Amascut (ToA) -- includes Akkha, Ba-Ba, Kephri, Zebak, Wardens
- **Location:** Jaltevas Pyramid, Necropolis, southern Kharidian Desert
- **Requirements:** Beneath Cursed Sands quest (partial completion for Necropolis access)
- **Drops:** Tumeken's Shadow, Masori armour, Elidinis' Ward, Lightbearer, Osmumten's Fang
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring AKP (Necropolis, requires 62 Agility). Pharaoh's Sceptre -> Jaltevas (once activated on obelisk). Magic carpet chain: Shantay Pass -> Pollnivneach -> Sophanem, walk SE.
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s from fairy ring AKP; ~10s from Pharaoh's Sceptre
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~5-15s from AKP; ~0-5s from Sceptre (Sceptre is nearly instant)

### 24. Kalphite Queen
- **Location:** Kalphite Lair, SW of Shantay Pass
- **Requirements:** 2 ropes (permanent with Hard Desert Diary)
- **Drops:** KQ head (diary), Dragon Chainbody, Dragon 2H Sword
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring BIQ (near Kalphite Lair) -> run NW to entrance -> descend 2 levels. Desert Amulet 4 teleports directly to KQ lair entrance (Elite Desert Diary).
- **Travel time:** ~30-40s from BIQ (includes rope climbing); ~10s with Desert Amulet 4
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~20-30s from BIQ; ~0-5s with Desert Amulet 4

### 25. Echo Kalphite Queen
- **Location:** Same as Kalphite Queen, requires Desert echo orb
- **Requirements:** Echo orb from KQ kills
- **Drops:** Drygore Blowpipe
- **Best teleport:** Same as KQ
- **Travel time:** Same as KQ
- **Evil Eye saves:** Same as KQ

### 26. The Leviathan
- **Location:** The Scar (Abyssal Space), accessed via Catalytic Guardian in Temple of the Eye
- **Requirements:** Desert Treasure II quest
- **Drops:** Siren's Staff, Leviathan's Lure, Virtus robes (1/1,088 each), Awakener's Orb
- **Best teleport:** Ring of Shadows -> The Scar (requires scarred tablet from Leviathan, rare drop initially). Without Ring of Shadows: Amulet of the Eye -> Temple of the Eye -> Catalytic Guardian -> Scar -> run east to Wizard Persten -> rowboat. Minigame teleport (GotR) to Temple of the Eye also works. In Demonic Pacts League, a portal near Emir's Arena provides access.
- **Travel time:** ~10-15s with Ring of Shadows; ~45-60s without (Temple route involves multiple transitions)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~35-50s before Ring of Shadows; ~0-10s after

### 27. Tempoross
- **Location:** Tempoross Cove, east of Ruins of Unkah, western coast of Kharidian Desert
- **Requirements:** None
- **Drops:** Spirit Angler, Spirit Flakes, Tome of Water, Tiny Tempor pet
- **Best teleport:** Ferryman Sathwood's ferry south of Al Kharid bank (direct). Ring of Dueling -> Emir's Arena (Al Kharid) -> run south to ferry. Minigame teleport -> Tempoross (if unlocked).
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s from Al Kharid via ferry
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 28. Guardians of the Rift
- **Location:** Temple of the Eye (in Demonic Pacts League, accessible via portal near Mage Training Arena in Desert)
- **Requirements:** Temple of the Eye quest
- **Drops:** Outfit pieces, Abyssal Pearls, Rift Guardian pet (minigame, not really a boss)
- **Best teleport:** Minigame teleport -> Guardians of the Rift. Or portal near MTA in Desert (league-specific).
- **Travel time:** ~5-10s with minigame teleport
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0s (minigame teleport is faster or equal)

## KANDARIN

### 29. Thermonuclear Smoke Devil
- **Location:** Smoke Devil Dungeon, south of Castle Wars
- **Requirements:** 93 Slayer, on a smoke devil task
- **Drops:** Occult Necklace, Smoke Battlestaff, Dragon Chainbody, Pet
- **Best teleport:** Ring of Dueling -> Castle Wars, run south. Fairy ring BKP (just SW of dungeon, very close).
- **Travel time:** ~20-30s from Castle Wars; ~10-15s from fairy ring BKP
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~5-10s from BKP (fairly close already)

### 30. Echo Thermonuclear Smoke Devil
- **Location:** Same as Thermy, requires Kandarin echo orb
- **Requirements:** 93 Slayer (task waived once echo orb obtained)
- **Drops:** Devil's Element (1/25, guaranteed at 50 KC)
- **Best teleport:** Same as Thermy
- **Travel time:** Same as Thermy
- **Evil Eye saves:** Same as Thermy

### 31. Kraken
- **Location:** Kraken Cove, SW of Piscatoris Fishing Colony
- **Requirements:** 87 Slayer, on a cave kraken task
- **Drops:** Trident of the Seas, Kraken Tentacle, Jar of Dirt, Pet
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring AKQ (Piscatoris Hunter area) -> run west to Kraken Cove entrance. Western Banner 3/4 teleport to Piscatoris also works.
- **Travel time:** ~15-25s from fairy ring AKQ
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 32. Demonic Gorillas
- **Location:** Crash Site Cavern, north of Tree Gnome Stronghold
- **Requirements:** Monkey Madness II quest
- **Drops:** Zenyte Shard, Ballista pieces, Monkey Tail
- **Best teleport:** Spirit tree -> Gnome Stronghold -> run NW through fence gap -> NE to cavern entrance. Royal Seed Pod -> Grand Tree base (fastest, MM2 reward). Gnome Glider -> Ta Quir Priw (top of Grand Tree).
- **Travel time:** ~20-30s from Spirit tree; ~15-20s from Royal Seed Pod
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~10-20s

### 33. Barbarian Assault (Penance Queen)
- **Location:** Barbarian Outpost, north of Baxtorian Falls
- **Requirements:** Team of 5 players (minigame)
- **Drops:** Fighter Torso, Penance equipment, Pet Penance Queen
- **Best teleport:** Games necklace -> Barbarian Outpost (direct). Minigame teleport -> Barbarian Assault. Barbarian Teleport (Lunar, level 75).
- **Travel time:** ~5-10s from Games necklace (direct teleport)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~0s (Games necklace is essentially instant)

### 34. Mithril Dragons
- **Location:** Ancient Cavern, beneath Baxtorian Falls (whirlpool entrance near Otto's Grotto)
- **Requirements:** Barbarian Training (Barbarian Firemaking portion)
- **Drops:** Dragon Full Helm, Chewed Bones, Draconic Visage
- **Best teleport:** Games necklace -> Barbarian Outpost, run west to whirlpool near Otto's Grotto -> dive in. Fairy ring BJR (near whirlpool? -- closest is Barbarian area).
- **Travel time:** ~30-40s from Games necklace (includes run + whirlpool dive)
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~20-30s (not on CA boss list -- Evil Eye may not teleport here directly, Mithril Dragons are notable Slayer monsters, not a CA boss)

## GENERAL (Available regardless of region)

### 35. Abyssal Sire
- **Location:** Abyssal Nexus, south of the Abyss (accessed via Fairy Ring DIP)
- **Requirements:** 85 Slayer, on an abyssal demon task, visited the Abyss at least once
- **Drops:** Unsired -> Abyssal Bludgeon, Abyssal Dagger, Abyssal Head
- **Best teleport:** Fairy ring DIP (direct to Abyssal Nexus, very close to Sire). This is already the standard method.
- **Travel time:** ~10-15s from fairy ring DIP
- **Evil Eye saves:** ~5-10s

## VERDICT: Is Evil Eye worth it over Map of Alacrity?

**For your build: No.** You chose Map of Alacrity at T3 (confirmed in HANDOFF.md). Here's why that's the right call:

1. **Most bosses have near-instant alternatives** -- CoX (Xeric's), Sol (Ring of Dueling), Wintertodt (Games necklace), Hespori (Skills necklace), GotR (minigame teleport)
2. **"Problem" bosses get self-solving drops** -- Vardorvis drops Ring of Shadows tablet (1/25), Doom drops Mokhaiotl Waystone, Leviathan drops scarred tablet, KQ is solved by Desert Amulet 4
3. **Hydra is the only persistent pain point** -- Deep dungeon every trip with no direct teleport item. But you only farm Hydra on Slayer task, and Map of Alacrity to the 88 Agility pipe shortcut inside the dungeon helps significantly
4. **Map of Alacrity provides utility everywhere** -- Agility shortcuts across all regions for Runecraft, general travel, and reaching bosses via shortcuts (Cam Torum for Moons, Hydra pipe, etc.)
5. **Sarachnis/Moons of Peril** are the other moderate losses, but neither is a long-term farm target for your mage build

The bosses you farm the most (CoX, ToA, Doom, Slayer bosses) all have good teleport alternatives. Evil Eye's value is in convenience across many bosses, but Map of Alacrity's value is in everything else too -- skilling, Runecraft loops, general travel.
