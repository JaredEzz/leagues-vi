**Target:** 99 | **Start:** Level 3 (+10 Abundance boost = effective 13)

## Primary Method: Mastering Mixology

**Location:** Aldarin, Varlamore (available from start)
**Requirement:** 60 Herblore (50 with Abundance boost)

### How It Works
1. Refine herbs into three paste types (mox, aga, lye)
2. Mix pastes to fill assigned potion orders
3. Process and deposit finished potions

### Rewards
Earns resin currencies for cosmetics and utility items.

## Getting to Level 60 (50 with Abundance)

- Start at level 3 (effective 13 with Abundance)
- Make basic potions from herbs you farm (1-min farming ticks = constant herb supply)
- Varlamore herb patches + Farming Guild herb patches (Kourend)
- Some secondaries from NPC shops (non-depleting in leagues)
- Moonlight potions and other Varlamore-specific recipes

## Alternative Methods

| Method | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Traditional potion making | Farm herbs (1-min ticks), gather/buy secondaries |
| Monster drops | Herbs from Slayer tasks |
| Farming Guild | Herb patches + contracts sometimes reward herb seeds |

## Notes

- Abundance gives +10 boost — effectively 10 levels ahead for recipe access
- Mixology is entirely in Varlamore, no region unlock needed
- 1-min farming ticks mean herb supply is never a bottleneck
- GP from Abundance helps buy whatever secondaries shops stock
