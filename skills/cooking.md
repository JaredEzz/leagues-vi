# Cooking -- Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Passive via Hotfoot (fish what you catch) | **Effectively FREE**

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~3.75 hrs |
| With Hotfoot only | **~2-3 hrs** (auto-cook while Fishing, 100% success rate) |
| With EH + Hotfoot + Flow State | **~1.5 hrs** (fish anglerfish with diabolic worms = ~8.7M Cooking XP/hr at 16x) |
| AFK karambwan (EH + Hotfoot) | **~2.8 hrs** (190 Cooking XP/fish, AFK, spot never moves) |

## How It Works With Relics

**Hotfoot gives searing boots:** Auto-cooks every fish you catch. 100% success rate (no burns, ever). No fire/range needed.

**Endless Harvest doubles fish:** 2x fish per catch. Both fish are auto-cooked by Hotfoot = 2x Cooking XP per catch action.

**Flow State:** 2-tick fishing = 2.5x more catch actions per hour = 2.5x more cooking actions.

**Combined:** Every 1.2 seconds you attempt a catch. When successful, you get 2 fish, both auto-cooked. Cooking is completely passive -- just fish.

## Strategy

### Fastest to 99 Cooking
Fish anglerfish with diabolic worms at Port Piscarilius (82+ Fishing, 84+ Cooking). At 16x multiplier with all relics: ~8.7M Cooking XP/hr. You hit 99 Cooking in ~1.5 hours of fishing.

### Most AFK to 99 Cooking
Fish karambwan at fairy ring DKP (65+ Fishing, 30+ Cooking). Spot never moves. At 16x with all relics: ~4.6M Cooking XP/hr. 99 Cooking in ~2.8 hours of AFK fishing.

### Early Game (pre-Hotfoot)
Cook fish manually on a fire/range. Trout and salmon from fly fishing. Quick with multiplier.

### After Hotfoot (T2)
Never manually cook again. Just fish with searing boots equipped. All cooking is passive.
