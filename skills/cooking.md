# Cooking — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Any (cook what you fish)

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~3.75 hrs |
| With Abundance only | **~3.5 hrs** (fewer burns, higher recipes earlier) |
| With Abundance + Hotfoot | **0-1 hrs** (auto-cook while Fishing — ~10-11M passive XP, top off last ~2-3M) |
| With Abundance + Woodsman | ~3.5 hrs (Woodsman doesn't affect Cooking) |

**Abundance saves ~15 min** (+10 boost = fewer burns, earlier recipe access). +2/drop = +1.1% (negligible).

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-30 | Cook meat/fish from early combat/fishing | Any | Quick with multiplier |
| 30-99 | Cook Karambwans / Barb Fishing drops | Karamja / Kandarin | ~3.6M XP/hr at 16x. Cook as you catch |

## Notes

- Ironman mode — can't buy raw fish. Cook what you catch
- Karambwans give ~190 base Cooking XP each, ~1,200 cooks/hr = ~3.6M/hr at 16x
- Abundance +10 boost = fewer burns, access to higher recipes earlier
- With Hotfoot: auto-cook while Fishing = Cooking is basically free
