## Key Mechanic: 1-Minute Farming Ticks

Farming ticks every 1 minute (instead of 5) in leagues. This means:
- Tree patches grow 5x faster
- Herb runs can be done constantly
- Allotments grow almost instantly

## Early Farming Strategy: Snape Grass (Transmuted Potatoes)

**The play:** Buy potato seeds from NPC shops → transmute up the allotment chain to snape grass seeds → plant at allotment patches → 14-min cycles with massive Nature's Accord 10x XP bonus.

### Why Snape Grass

- **Allotment patch** (no special patch needed)
- **82 XP plant + 82 XP harvest per item**
- **3 seeds per patch** = 246 plant XP + ~2,460 harvest XP (with NA 10x yield) = **~2,706 base XP per patch**
- **70 min growth → ~14 min in leagues** (1-min ticks vs 5-min)
- Seeds are CHEAP and INFINITE via Transmutation from potato seeds

### Requirements

- **51 real Farming** (61 effective with Abundance) to plant snape grass
- OR **Nature's Accord T5** which removes Farming level requirements entirely (plant from level 1)
- Transmutation T4 for seed upgrades

### Transmute Chain

Allotment seeds in chain order: **Potato → Onion → Cabbage → Tomato → Sweetcorn → Strawberry → Watermelon → Snape grass**

That's **7 transmute steps** from potato to snape grass.

For 30 seeds (10 patches' worth):
- 7 casts × 3s each = **21 seconds total** (each cast handles 10 items)
- Trivial overhead

## Recommended Progression

1. **Phase 1-2 (pre-Nature's Accord):** Hit 51 real Farming via early planting (basic crops). Once Transmutation unlocks (T4), start snape grass loop with 51+ real Farming
2. **Nature's Accord (T5):** No level reqs anymore — snape grass loop fully active. Pump XP via 5+ allotment patches
3. **Transition to tree seeds:** Once you have farmer's fortune outfit (Tithe Farm) and tree seeds from Hespori/contracts, switch to tree runs for late-game XP
4. **Endgame:** Magic trees, redwoods, celastrus from contracts

## Tithe Farm (Backup)

Located in Farming Guild (Kourend). Minigame-style Farming training. Decent XP and earns points for seed packs + the Farmer's outfit (+2.5% Farming XP). With 4x minigame points in leagues, this is efficient.

## Notes

- Farming Guild is the hub — contracts, Hespori, Master Farmer, patches all in one place
- 1-minute ticks = do a tree run, go do a Slayer task, come back and trees are done
- Abundance +10 boost = access to higher-tier patches earlier
- **Snape grass via Transmutation = best early-mid Farming method**, doesn't require Hespori or contracts
- Nature's Accord fairy mushroom teleports to tool leprechauns = direct allotment access
