# Mining — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Varlamore

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-41 | Standard rocks / copper-tin | Varlamore | Quick with XP multiplier |
| 41-99 | Calcified Rocks (Cam Torum) | Varlamore | AFK, good XP |
| Any | Shooting Stars | Varlamore (land anywhere) | Very AFK, land in accessible regions |

## Notes

- Entirely trainable in Varlamore from start
- Abundance +10 boost = effective 10 levels higher for ore access
- Shooting Stars are great for AFK sessions between active training
- No region unlock needed
