# Attack — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-50 | Sulphur Naguas (AFK) | Varlamore | Near-best melee XP, very AFK |
| 50-99 | Slayer (Catacombs of Kourend) | Kourend | Train Attack through Slayer tasks. Multi-combat for efficiency |

## Notes

- Swap to Attack style while doing Slayer for passive training
- Abundance +10 doesn't apply to combat skills
- Tier 3 relic gives 1.5x combat XP multiplier on top of base XP rate
