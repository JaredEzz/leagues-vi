# Firemaking — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Kourend

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~2.75 hrs |
| With Abundance only | **~2.5 hrs** (Wintertodt at 40 real. Many XP drops per game = Abundance +2/drop adds up slightly) |
| With Abundance + Hotfoot | ~2.5 hrs (Hotfoot doesn't affect FM) |
| With Abundance + Woodsman | **0 hrs** (logs auto-burn during WC — FM trains passively while doing Redwoods) |

**Abundance saves ~15 min** (+10 boost = Wintertodt at 40 instead of 50). Wintertodt has many small XP drops per game (log adds, kindling, lighting), so Abundance +2/drop is slightly better here than most skills, but still marginal.

**Wintertodt + Abundance at 16x = ~4-6M FM XP/hr.** This is one of the fastest 99s in the league. Quick and painless.

## Primary Method: Wintertodt

**Location:** Northern Kourend
**Requirement:** 50 Firemaking (40 real with Abundance), rune axe for efficiency
**HP doesn't matter** — warmth meter replaced HP damage (Oct 2024 update). Do it whenever.

## Why Wintertodt

- 8x minigame points at Tier 4
- Great Firemaking XP (~4-6M/hr at 16x)
- **Rewards scale with skill levels at time of opening** — higher skills = better loot
- Points for Pyromancer outfit (+2.5% FM XP)
- Relatively AFK
- Can be done at any point once Kourend is unlocked + you have a rune axe

## Reward Scaling — When to Open Carts

Rewards are determined when you **open** the cart, not when you earn it. Strategy: bank carts, train skills, then open for best loot.

| Skill | Optimal Level (real / effective) | What It Gives |
|-------|--------------------------------|---------------|
| **Farming** | **67 real (77 effective)** | Best seeds — tree seeds, herb seeds. Feeds Farming contracts + Nature's Accord 10x yield |
| **Herblore** | **56 real (66 effective)** | Best herbs — ranarr, snapdragon, etc. Direct Herblore supply |
| **Woodcutting** | Higher = better | Magic logs, yew logs |
| **Mining** | Higher = better | Runite, adamantite ore |
| **Fishing** | Higher = better | Sharks, etc. |
| **Crafting** | Higher = better | Better gems |

**Ideal timing:** Do Wintertodt whenever for FM XP, but **don't open carts until Farming 67+ and Herblore 56+** (real levels, Abundance adds +10). This maximizes seed/herb rewards.

## Notes

- **HP doesn't matter anymore** — warmth meter, not HP-based damage
- Get a rune axe before starting (faster chopping = more points per game)
- Do WT whenever for FM XP, bank carts, open later with high skills for best rewards
- Abundance +10 = accessible at 40 FM real
- With Woodsman relic: skip WT entirely — FM is free from WC. But you miss out on supply crate rewards
- Forestry events in Varlamore also give some FM XP
