# Firemaking — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Kourend

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~2.75 hrs |
| With Abundance only | **~2.5 hrs** (Wintertodt at 40 real. Many XP drops per game = Abundance +2/drop adds up slightly) |
| With Abundance + Hotfoot | ~2.5 hrs (Hotfoot doesn't affect FM) |
| With Abundance + Woodsman | **0 hrs** (logs auto-burn during WC — FM trains passively while doing Redwoods) |

**Abundance saves ~15 min** (+10 boost = Wintertodt at 40 instead of 50). Wintertodt has many small XP drops per game (log adds, kindling, lighting), so Abundance +2/drop is slightly better here than most skills, but still marginal.

**Wintertodt + Abundance at 16x = ~4-6M FM XP/hr.** This is one of the fastest 99s in the league. Quick and painless.

## Primary Method: Wintertodt

**Location:** Northern Kourend
**Requirement:** 50 Firemaking (40 with Abundance boost)

## Why Wintertodt

- 8x minigame points at Tier 4
- Great Firemaking XP (~4-6M/hr at 16x)
- Rewards: seeds (feed Nature's Accord), herbs (for Herblore), ores, logs
- Points for Pyromancer outfit (+2.5% FM XP)
- Relatively AFK

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-50 | Burn logs from WC training / Forestry | Varlamore | Quick with multiplier |
| 50-99 | Wintertodt | Kourend | Best method. AFK + supplies + minigame points |

## Notes

- Wintertodt scales with HP — lower HP = less damage taken. Consider doing early
- Abundance +10 = accessible at 40 FM
- Forestry events in Varlamore also give some FM XP
- With Woodsman relic: skip Wintertodt entirely — FM is free from Woodcutting
