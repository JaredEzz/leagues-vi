# Fishing — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kandarin, Karamja, Desert | **Mostly AFK**

## Progression Plan

### Phase 1: Angler's Outfit (Fishing Trawler)

**Location:** Port Khazard (Kandarin)
**Requirement:** 15 Fishing for rewards
**Drop rate:** ~1/12 per piece per game (4 pieces total)

Get this first — the outfit gives +2.5% Fishing XP bonus. With 4x minigame points in leagues, this goes fast. 5-minute games.

### Phase 2: AFK Fishing

| Method | Region | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Karambwans** | Karamja | Main AFK method. Cook for Cooking XP |
| Barbarian Fishing | Kandarin | If you want active XP + passive Agility/Strength |

### Phase 3: Spirit Angler's Outfit (Tempoross)

**Location:** Ruins of Unkah (Desert)
**Requirement:** 35 Fishing

Recolor Angler's outfit into Spirit Angler's using Tempoross reward points. With 4x (8x at Tier 4) minigame points, this is quick. Tempoross also gives:
- Supplies (fish, planks, etc.)
- Tiny Tempor pet chance
- Spirit Flakes (+50% extra fish when fishing)

## Notes

- Angler's outfit first (Trawler, Kandarin) → AFK Karambwans (Karamja) → Spirit Angler recolor (Tempoross, Desert)
- Cook Karambwans as you catch them for Cooking XP
- Abundance +10 boost = access to higher fish earlier
- Mostly AFK skill — do between active training sessions
