# Strength — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-50 | Sulphur Naguas (AFK) | Varlamore | Swap to Strength style |
| 50-99 | Slayer (Catacombs of Kourend) | Kourend | Train Strength through Slayer on Strength style |

## Notes

- Same training locations as Attack, just swap combat style
- Tier 3 relic gives 1.5x combat XP multiplier
