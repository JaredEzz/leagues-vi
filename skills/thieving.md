# Thieving — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kandarin, Desert, Varlamore

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~4 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~3 hrs** (+10 boost → Ardy Knights at 45 real, higher success rates everywhere) |

**Abundance saves ~1 hr** (+10 boost = better success rates = fewer fails = faster XP).

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-45 | Pickpocketing citizens / cake stalls | Varlamore / Kandarin | Early levels |
| 45-55 | Blackjacking (Pollnivneach) | Desert | Bandits/thugs. High APM but fast XP |
| 55-65 | Ardy Knights | Kandarin | Classic AFK method, 55+ Thieving |
| 65-91 | Ardy Knights / Bazaar pickpocketing | Ka / V | Street Urchin windows = 100% success in Bazaar |
| 91-99 | Gnomes (Tree Gnome Stronghold?) | Kandarin | Need to confirm access |

## Alternative Methods

| Method | Region | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Bazaar pickpocketing (Street Urchin) | Varlamore | 100% success windows, very chill |
| Master Farmer | Kourend (Farming Guild) | Seeds for Farming + Thieving XP |
| Blackjacking | Desert (Pollnivneach) | Fastest raw XP if you don't mind the clicks |

## Notes

- Abundance +10 boost means you're effectively 10 levels ahead for success rates
- Ardy Knights are the classic go-to for consistent XP
- Blackjacking in Pollnivneach is faster but more click-intensive
- Bazaar in Varlamore is the most AFK option with Street Urchin mechanic
