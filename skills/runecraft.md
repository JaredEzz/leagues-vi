# Runecraft — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend | **Start:** Level 5

**Key relics:** Map of Alacrity (T3) + Flow State (T7) + Endless Harvest (T1)

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~5 hrs |
| With MoA + Flow State + EH | **~2.5-3.5 hrs** (shortcut teleports skip return runs, 2-tick actions if applicable, 2x essence) |

**No Abundance** — need 77 RC for Bloods (not 67). ~30-45 min extra early leveling at 8x multiplier.

## Phase 1: Getting to Sunfire (Level 5 → 23 real / 33 effective)

**The Abyss is available in all regions** in this league — Jagex confirmed this. Exact access method TBD (likely relocated NPC or portal in starting area — check day 1 / Doubleshine route).

The Abyss gives access to every runecraft altar regardless of region. So from Varlamore:

| Level (real / eff) | Method | Notes |
|-------------------|--------|-------|
| 5 (15 eff) | Abyss → Air/Mind/Water runes | Craft whatever you can. ~20-30 min at 5x to reach 23 real |
| 5 (15 eff) | Quest XP rewards | Some quests grant RC XP. Auto-completed quests from region unlocks may help |
| 5 (15 eff) | Task reward lamps | League task reward XP |

**XP needed:** Level 5 → 23 = ~6,200 XP. At 5x multiplier = ~1,240 base XP. Trivial — a few inventories of runes at any altar via the Abyss.

**Essence source:** Monster drops (most monsters drop pure essence), or mine essence at rune/pure essence rocks.

## Phase 2: Sunfire Runecrafting (23 → 67 real / 33 → 77 effective)

**Location:** Shrine of Ralos, Varlamore
**Requirement:** 33 RC (23 real with Abundance)

### How Sunfire Runes Work

Each craft requires:
- 1 fire rune
- 1 sunfire splinter
- 1 pure essence (or daeyalt essence for 1.5x XP)

**XP per rune:** 9 base (13.5 with daeyalt essence)

### Output Scaling

| RC Level (real / eff) | Runes per Essence | Effect |
|----------------------|-------------------|--------|
| 23 (33 eff) | 1 | Base rate |
| 39 (49 eff) | **2** | Double output — same inputs, 2x runes |
| 88 (98 eff) | **3** | Triple output |

Doubling at 39 real is a big jump — effectively doubles your XP/hr for the same supply cost.

### Supply Sources (All Varlamore)

| Material | Source | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Fire runes** | Craft at fire altar via Abyss, or buy from rune shops | Rune shops non-depleting in leagues |
| **Sunfire splinters** | Sunlight Antelope (72 Hunter, 2-6 each) | Also your Hunter training |
| | Hueycoatl boss (17-350 per drop) | Varlamore boss |
| | Fortis Colosseum wave rewards | Sol Heredit practice |
| | Moon key chests (250 each) | Moons of Peril |
| **Pure essence** | Monster drops (very common from Slayer) | Banks up passively |
| | Mine essence rocks | Slower, use as last resort |

**Supply bottleneck:** Sunfire splinters early on. You won't have 72 Hunter immediately for Sunlight Antelope. Early splinter sources:
- Hueycoatl kills (17-350 per drop, Varlamore boss)
- Colosseum wave rewards
- Moon key chests from Moons of Peril

Once Hunter hits 62 real (72 eff), Sunlight Antelope hunting gives steady splinters AND Hunter XP.

### XP Rates

Hard to pin exact XP/hr — depends on how fast you can bank/craft and your splinter supply. Roughly:
- ~30-50k base XP/hr (varies with level and supply flow)
- At 16x: ~480k-800k/hr
- At 8x: ~240k-400k/hr

Sunfire RC is steady but not blazing fast. It's the best available in Varlamore before Blood runes.

## Phase 3: Blood & Soul Runes (77+ real)

**Location:** Arceuus, Kourend
**Requirement:** 77 RC for Blood, 90 RC for Soul (no Abundance +10 — need real levels)

### Why Blood/Soul Runes

- Very AFK — mine dark essence, chisel into fragments, craft at altar
- Good XP (38k-45k base/hr for bloods, more for souls)
- At 16x: ~600k-720k/hr
- Profitable (runes sell well, useful for your own spellcasting)
- Much more chill than Sunfire (no splinter supply management)

### The Map of Alacrity Shortcut Loop

The normal Zeah RC loop:
1. Mine dense essence blocks at the mine
2. Run west to Dark Altar → convert to dark essence blocks
3. Chisel blocks into fragments while running
4. Run south to Blood Altar (or east to Soul Altar) → craft runes
5. **Run all the way back to the mine** ← normally the slow part

**With Map of Alacrity, step 5 is eliminated:**
- **Blood runes:** Teleport to **Western Rocks shortcut (73 Agi, bypassed)** → drop into mine instantly. Skips the entire Blood Altar → mine return run.
- **Soul runes:** Teleport to **Boulder shortcut (49 Agi, bypassed)** → jump down into mine from Soul Altar area. Or use **Eastern Rocks (52)** / **Northern Rocks (69)** for two-way traversal.

All 4 shortcuts around the dense essence mine:

| Shortcut | Agility Req (bypassed by MoA) | Direction | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Western Rocks** | 73 | One-way (Blood Altar → Mine) | **Primary blood rune shortcut.** Teleport here after crafting, drop into mine. |
| **Boulder** | 49 | One-way (Soul Altar → Mine) | **Primary soul rune shortcut.** Jump down after crafting souls. |
| **Eastern Rocks** | 52 | Two-way | Alternative Soul Altar route |
| **Northern Rocks** | 69 | Two-way | Alternative Soul Altar route |

### Relic Synergy (Needs Day 1 Testing)

| Relic | Expected Effect | Certainty |
|---|---|---|
| **Map of Alacrity** | Teleport to shortcut, skip return run | **Guaranteed** |
| **Endless Harvest** | 2x dense essence blocks per mine, infinite runestone (no depletion) | **Uncertain** — dense runestone is special (Mining cape / Varrock armour don't work on it). EH auto-bank will fail since dense essence blocks can't be banked — blocks go to inventory instead. Needs testing. |
| **Flow State** | 2-tick mining + 2-tick chiseling | **Uncertain** — dense runestone has a fixed 9-tick cycle that may override Flow State. Chiseling may or may not count as a Crafting action for Flow State. Needs testing. |

**Guaranteed value:** Map of Alacrity shortcuts alone significantly speed up the loop by cutting the return run.

**Best case (if EH + Flow State both work):** Mine 2x blocks at 2-tick speed without depletion → teleport skip return → chisel at 2-tick → craft. Could roughly double trips/hr AND double blocks/trip = ~4x the normal output.

**Worst case (if dense runestone is special-cased):** Normal mining speed, normal block count, but still get the MoA shortcut teleports. Still faster than base.

### Blood Runes (77+ real)

1. Mine dense essence at the mine
2. Run west to Dark Altar → convert to dark essence blocks
3. Chisel into fragments while running south
4. Craft at Blood Altar (23.8 RC XP per fragment)
5. **MoA teleport to Western Rocks → drop into mine**
6. ~38,000 base XP/hr → **~608k/hr at 16x** (potentially much higher with EH/Flow State)

### Soul Runes (90+ real)

1. Same process, Soul Altar is east of the mine
2. **MoA teleport to Boulder → jump into mine**
3. ~45,000 base XP/hr → **~720k/hr at 16x**
4. Soul runes are useful for casting spells (barrage costs souls)

## Alternative: Ourania/ZMI Altar (Kandarin, Phase 4)

**Location:** West of Battlefield, south of West Ardougne
**XP/hr:** ~42k-77k base depending on level → 672k-1.2M at 16x

Ourania is faster than Blood runes but only available from Phase 4 (Kandarin). If you still need RC XP after unlocking Kandarin, ZMI is the fastest non-lava option. But by Phase 4 you'll likely be at or near 99 from Blood/Soul runes.

## Recommended Progression

1. **5-23 real:** Abyss → basic runes. Quick at 5x multiplier (~20-30 min)
2. **23-39 real:** Sunfire runes at Shrine of Ralos. Single output. Steady
3. **39-77 real:** Sunfire runes (doubled output at 49 eff!). XP effectively doubles. Longer phase without Abundance +10.
4. **77+ real:** **Blood runes** (Kourend) with MoA shortcut loop. AFK, chill, good XP
5. **90+ real:** **Soul runes** (Kourend) with MoA shortcut loop. Best AFK RC, useful runes for barraging

## Notes

- Start at level 5 in leagues (not level 1)
- Abyss confirmed available in all regions — exact access point TBD at launch
- Sunfire RC needs fire runes + splinters + essence. Splinters are the bottleneck early (before 72 Hunter)
- Blood/Soul at Kourend is the endgame — very AFK, switch as soon as you hit 77 real
- No Abundance +10 = need 77 real for Bloods (not 67), 90 real for Souls (not 80). ~30-45 min extra Sunfire phase
- Map of Alacrity shortcut teleports eliminate return runs in the Blood/Soul loop
- Ourania/ZMI (Kandarin, Phase 4) is faster than Bloods if you need a final push
- Test EH + Flow State on dense runestone Day 1 — if they work, RC becomes insane
