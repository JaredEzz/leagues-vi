# Runecraft — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~5 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~4 hrs** (+10 boost → Blood runes at 67 real, Sunfire RC at 23 real) |

**Abundance saves ~1 hr** (+10 boost = earlier access to Blood/Soul altars in Kourend).

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 5-33 | Basic runes (start at RC 5) | Varlamore | Quick with multiplier |
| 33-77 | Sunfire Runecrafting (Shrine of Ralos) | Varlamore | Doubled at 49, tripled at 98. Great XP |
| 77-99 | Blood runes / Soul runes | Kourend | Blood altar (77 RC), Soul altar (90 RC). AFK, good XP |

## Notes

- Start at level 5 in leagues (not level 1)
- Sunfire RC is excellent and entirely in Varlamore
- Blood/Soul rune crafting in Kourend is very AFK for endgame
- Abundance +10 boost = Blood runes at 67 effective RC, Soul runes at 80
- Soul runes needed for Elidinis' Ward (f) crafting (10,000 soul runes) — but ward fortification is locked (Corp)
