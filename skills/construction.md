## GP Situation

Abundance relic generates 2 coins per XP earned — it's not unlimited GP, it scales with how much you train. Construction is expensive (planks), so you may need to bank GP from other training before dumping it into Construction.

**Rough math:** To get 99 Construction you need ~30M+ XP worth of planks. At high-tier planks (mahogany = ~1,500 gp each), that's a significant GP investment. You'll want to stockpile GP from other training first.

## Methods

### Vale Totems (Auburnvale, Varlamore)
- Trains Construction AND Fletching simultaneously
- Good early-mid option
- Doesn't require planks/GP

### Plank Methods (Traditional)
1. Chop logs (Forestry in Varlamore, Redwoods in Kourend for other WC training)
2. Sawmill at Auburnvale (closest to bank in Varlamore)
3. Build + remove furniture in POH
4. Mahogany tables / Mythical capes (if quest access) for fast XP

### Log Sources
- Auburnvale (Varlamore) — Forestry for various logs
- Kourend — trees + potential shop stock
- Buy logs from NPC shops where available (non-depleting in leagues)

## Recommended Progression

1. **1-33:** Vale Totems (Varlamore) — no GP needed, trains Fletching too
2. **33-50:** Continue Vale Totems or start basic plank furniture
3. **50-99:** Oak/Teak/Mahogany plank methods in POH. Sawmill at Auburnvale. Stockpile GP from other skills first.

## Notes

- Need 83 Crafting + 70 Smithing for Confliction Gauntlets — plan Construction around this
- Abundance +10 boost means effective level is always 10 higher
- Vale Totems are a great early option that doesn't drain your GP reserves
- Consider training other skills first to bank GP, then dump into Construction
