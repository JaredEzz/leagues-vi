# Prayer — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Varlamore

## Primary Method: Ralos' Rise

**Location:** Varlamore (available from start)
**Requirement:** 30 Prayer
**XP Rate:** Up to 1.2M XP/hr (base) — best Prayer method in the entire game

### How It Works
Collect Blessed Bone Shards → use at Libation Bowl for massive Prayer XP.

## Notes

- This is the single best Prayer training method in OSRS, period
- Entirely in Varlamore, no region unlock needed
- At 16x XP multiplier this is absurdly fast
- Augury (BIS mage prayer) comes from CoX (Kourend), not Prayer training
