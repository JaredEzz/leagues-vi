## Primary Method: Cut Rubies (Toci's Gem Store, Varlamore)

**Location:** Aldarin Market, Varlamore — **available from Phase 1!**
**Requirement:** 63 Crafting (53 real with Abundance) for rubies
**Shop:** Non-depleting in leagues = infinite supply

### Stock Reality Check

Toci's base stock (non-depleting = buy infinite at base stock level):
- Uncut sapphire: **3** in stock ✅
- Uncut emerald: **2** in stock ✅
- Uncut ruby: **1** in stock ✅
- Uncut diamond: **0** in stock ❌ (2-hr restock, can't buy in bulk)

**Diamonds are NOT bulk-buyable.** Rubies are the highest gem you can buy infinitely.

### The Loop (GP Positive!)

1. Buy uncut rubies from Toci's: **100 gp each**
2. Cut them: **85 Crafting XP each**
3. Sell cut rubies back to Toci's: **700 gp each** (70% of 1,000)
4. **Profit: +600 gp per gem**

You MAKE money training Crafting. Abundance GP seeds the first batch, then the profit loop sustains itself.

## Recommended Progression

1. **1-7 real (17 eff):** Cut sapphires from Toci's. Quick, GP positive
2. **7-43 real (53 eff):** Cut emeralds from Toci's. GP positive
3. **43-99 real:** **Cut rubies** from Toci's. 3.8M/hr at 16x. GP positive the whole way
4. Optional: swap to d'hide bodies at 67+ real if you have a big stack of hides from Slayer
5. Cut any diamonds that trickle in from Toci's restock (1 every 2 hrs) or from drops — they're better XP

## Key Milestones

| Level (real) | Unlock |

## Notes

- Toci's Gem Store is in **Varlamore** — no region unlock needed!
- Al Kharid gem trader (Desert) has same gems, same 0 diamond stock, buys at 65% instead of 70% — Toci's is better
- Non-depleting shops in leagues = infinite rubies (1 base stock = infinite)
- Diamonds have 0 base stock — only trickle in on 2-hr restock. Not bulk-buyable
- GP positive loop: buy uncut ruby 100 → cut → sell 700 → repeat
- Abundance +10 = rubies accessible at 43 real Crafting (53 effective)
- Abundance GP seeds the initial purchases, then the profit loop is self-sustaining
