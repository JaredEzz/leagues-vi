# Crafting — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 (need 83 for Confliction Gauntlets) | **Regions:** Desert, Any

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~5 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~2 hrs** (+10 boost → Confliction craftable at 73 real. GP buys gems from Al Kharid, saves ~2 hrs gathering) |

**Abundance saves ~3 hrs** (biggest saver: GP funding gem purchases from Al Kharid gem trader, eliminating gathering time. +10 boost gives earlier access to higher-tier crafting).

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-20 | Leather crafting (cowhides from combat) | Any | Basic start |
| 20-63 | Gem cutting (buy gems from Al Kharid gem trader) | Desert | NPC shop, non-depleting in leagues. Abundance GP |
| 63-99 | Green/Blue d'hide bodies or gem cutting | Any | Hides from dragon Slayer tasks. Gems from shop |

## Key Milestone

**83 Crafting** — required to craft Confliction Gauntlets (+ 70 Smithing)

## Gem Sources

| Source | Region | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Al Kharid gem trader | Desert | Buy uncut gems with Abundance GP |
| Gemstone Crabs | Varlamore | Drop gems as part of the fight |
| Mining (gem rocks) | Various | Occasional gem drops while mining |

## Notes

- Al Kharid gem trader is the main supplier — Desert region covers this
- Abundance +10 boost = effective 10 levels ahead
- Prioritize hitting 83 early for Confliction Gauntlets
