# Crafting — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 (need 83 for Confliction Gauntlets) | **Region:** Varlamore (Toci's from start!)

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Cut diamonds at 16x | **~2.7 hrs** |
| With Abundance | **~2.7 hrs** (+2/drop = +1.9%, saves ~3 min. +10 boost = diamonds at 33 real) |
| D'hide bodies (if supply exists) | ~1.9-2.6 hrs (faster but need hides from Slayer) |

## Primary Method: Cut Diamonds (Toci's Gem Store, Varlamore)

**Location:** Aldarin Market, Varlamore — **available from Phase 1!**
**Requirement:** 43 Crafting (33 real with Abundance) for diamonds
**Shop:** Non-depleting in leagues = infinite supply

### The Loop (GP Positive!)

1. Buy uncut diamonds from Toci's: **200 gp each**
2. Cut them: **107.5 Crafting XP each**
3. Sell cut diamonds back to Toci's: **1,400 gp each** (70% of 2,000)
4. **Profit: +1,200 gp per gem**

### Math to 99

| | Value |
|---|-------|
| XP/hr (base) | 298,850 |
| XP/hr at 16x | **~4.8M** |
| Diamonds needed to 99 | ~7,558 |
| Buy cost | 1.5M gp |
| Sell revenue | 10.6M gp |
| **Net profit** | **+9.1M GP** |
| Time | **~2.7 hours** |

You MAKE money training Crafting. Abundance GP seeds the first batch, then the profit loop sustains itself.

## Alternative: D'hide Bodies (If You Have Supply)

| D'hide | Crafting Lvl (real) | Base XP/hr | At 16x | Supply |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|--------|--------|
| Green | 67 real (77 eff) | 313k | 5M | Green d'hides from dragon Slayer tasks |

## Recommended Progression

1. **1-7 real (17 eff):** Cut sapphires from Toci's. Quick, GP positive
2. **7-33 real (43 eff):** Cut emeralds from Toci's. GP positive
3. **33-99 real:** **Cut diamonds** from Toci's. 4.8M/hr at 16x. GP positive the whole way
4. Optional: swap to d'hide bodies at 67+ real if you have a big stack of hides from Slayer

## Key Milestones

| Level (real) | Unlock |
|-------------|--------|
| 33 (43 eff) | Diamond cutting — main method starts |
| 53 (63 eff) | Can cut dragonstones (if you find any from drops) |
| 73 (83 eff) | **Confliction Gauntlets craftable** (+ 60 real Smithing) |

## Notes

- Toci's Gem Store is in **Varlamore** — no region unlock needed!
- Al Kharid gem trader (Desert) has same gems but buys at 65% instead of 70% — Toci's is better
- Non-depleting shops in leagues = infinite diamonds
- GP positive loop: buy uncut 200 → cut → sell 1,400 → repeat
- Abundance +10 = diamonds accessible at 33 real Crafting
- Abundance GP seeds the initial purchases, then the profit loop is self-sustaining
