# Magic — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kourend + Desert | **BUILD SKILL — mage main**

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~6 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~6 hrs** (Abundance +10 doesn't apply to combat skills) |

**Abundance saves ~5 min**. Combat skill — no +10 boost. +2/drop = +0.5% (negligible). Magic trains passively through barrage Slayer anyway.

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-55 | Combat spells / Slayer | Varlamore | Standard spellbook early |
| 55-99 | Ice Burst/Barrage Slayer in Catacombs | Ko + D | Dust Devils (65 Slayer), Nechryaels (80 Slayer). Fastest mage XP in game |

## Key Unlocks

- **Ancient Magicks** — Desert quest unlock. Ice Burst (70 Magic), Ice Barrage (94 Magic)
- **Kodai Wand** — autocasts Ancients, +15% magic dmg, unlimited water runes (CoX, Kourend)
- **Augury** — BIS mage prayer (CoX, Kourend)

## Barrage Slayer Details

Multi-combat in Catacombs of Kourend. Stack monsters, Ice Barrage for AoE.
- **Dust Devils** (65 Slayer) — best early barrage task
- **Nechryaels** (80 Slayer) — best overall barrage task
- Defensive casting trains Defence simultaneously

## Notes

- With Kodai Wand: unlimited water runes + 15% chance to negate rune cost = very sustainable
- Abundance +10 doesn't apply to Magic (combat skill)
- Tier 3 gives 1.5x combat XP multiplier
- Magic skillcape from Wizards' Guild (Yanille, Kandarin) — 5x/day spellbook swaps
