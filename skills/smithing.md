**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Desert (Giants' Foundry)

## Primary Method: Giants' Foundry (Buy Equipment, No Smelting)

**Location:** Giants' Plateau (Desert)
**Requirement:** Sleeping Giants quest. Bar tier determines Smithing level needed.
**Available from:** Phase 3 (Desert unlock)

### How It Works
1. Buy metal equipment from NPC shops (non-depleting in leagues)
2. Dump equipment into Giants' Foundry crucible (counts as bar equivalents)
3. Shape sword, hand in for XP. No smelting needed.

### Equipment Bar Values in Crucible

Items are worth **one less bar** than it takes to make them:

| Equipment Type | Bar Value in Crucible |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Platebody | 4 bars |
| Platelegs / Plateskirt / Chainbody / Kiteshield / 2H sword / Battleaxe / Warhammer | 2 bars |
| Scimitar / Longsword / Full helm / Square shield / Claws | 1 bar |
| Dart tips / Arrowtips / Nails / Daggers / Maces / Axes | 0 bars (useless) |

**Platebodies are best (4 bars) but no shop in your regions sells them.** Platelegs at 2 bars are your best option.

- 7 Mithril platelegs (14 mith bars) = 18,200 gp

## Recommended Progression

1. **Phase 1-2 (before Desert):** Anvil smithing in Varlamore with ores from Mining. Slow but multiplier helps. Just get levels passively.
2. **Phase 3 (Desert unlocked, 60 real Smithing):** Start Giants' Foundry with **Mith + Addy alloy** (Option A). Buy platelegs from Louie's.
3. **75 real Smithing (85 effective):** Switch to **Addy + Rune alloy** (Option B) if you want speed. Or stay on Option A if you don't want to travel to Nardah for rune chainbodies.
4. Need 60 real Smithing (70 effective) for Confliction Gauntlets crafting — hit this early.

## Notes

- **All shops non-depleting in leagues** = infinite supply
- Abundance GP easily covers all costs (1.9M-7.5M total is pocket change)
- Platelegs are the sweet spot — 2 bar value, affordable, available in your regions
- No platebody shop exists in your regions — platelegs are the best you can buy
- Black equipment CANNOT be used in Giants' Foundry
- Dragon equipment CANNOT be used in Giants' Foundry
- Can also feed in Slayer drops and random smithable equipment you pick up
