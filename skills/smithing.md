# Smithing — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Kourend

## Primary Method: Blast Furnace

**Location:** Kelvian (Kourend)
**Requirement:** 60 Smithing for access (50 with Abundance boost)

Blast Furnace ore shop sells ores (non-depleting in leagues). Buy ores with Abundance GP → smelt bars → smith items.

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-30 | Bronze/Iron items (Varlamore anvils) | Varlamore | Use ores from Mining training |
| 30-60 | Steel/Mithril bars at Blast Furnace | Kourend | Once Kourend unlocked |
| 60-99 | Gold bars → Adamant/Rune bars at BF | Kourend | Gold bars with goldsmith gauntlets if available. Otherwise addy/rune |

## Notes

- Blast Furnace halves coal requirements = big efficiency boost
- Abundance GP covers ore shop purchases
- Need 70 Smithing for Confliction Gauntlets crafting
- Abundance +10 boost = BF accessible at 50 Smithing
