# Smithing — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Desert

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~4.2 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~2.7 hrs** (+10 boost → higher tier bars earlier. GP buys ores from NPC shops) |

**Abundance saves ~1.5 hrs** (+10 boost + GP for ore purchases).

~~Blast Furnace~~ — **LOCKED.** BF is in Keldagrim (Fremennik). Not in your regions.

## Primary Method: Giants' Foundry

**Location:** Giants' Plateau (Desert)
**Requirement:** Sleeping Giants quest, no minimum Smithing level (bar tier determines level)

### How It Works
1. Select moulds matching the commission
2. Fill crucible with metal bars or smithed equipment
3. Shape the weapon using trip hammer, grindstone, polishing wheel
4. Hand in finished sword for Smithing XP + reward points

### XP Rates (Base)

| Bar Tier | Base XP/hr | At 16x |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Iron/Steel | ~85-90k | ~1.4M |
| Mithril/Adamant | ~135k | ~2.2M |
| Adamant/Rune | ~195k | ~3.1M |
| Rune | ~276k | **~4.4M** |

### Bar/Ore Sources

| Source | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| NPC ore shops | Buy with Abundance GP. Smelt at furnace |
| Mining drops | Ores from Mining training |
| Wintertodt carts | Ores from reward carts (scale with Mining level) |
| Slayer drops | Metal items + bars from monster drops |
| Recycle equipment | Feed unwanted smithed gear into the crucible |

## Alternative: Anvil Smithing

Standard anvil smithing in Varlamore. Slower than Giants' Foundry but available from Phase 1 (before Desert unlock).

## Recommended Progression

1. **1-30:** Anvil smithing in Varlamore with ores from Mining
2. **30+:** Giants' Foundry (once Desert is unlocked). Use best bars available
3. **75+ real (85 effective):** Rune bars at Giants' Foundry for max XP rates

## Notes

- Abundance +10 boost = effective level 10 higher for bar access
- Abundance GP funds ore purchases from NPC shops
- Giants' Foundry is in Desert — available from Phase 3 (2nd region unlock)
- Can also recycle dropped/smithed equipment for free Foundry materials
- Need 70 Smithing (60 real) for Confliction Gauntlets crafting
