# Woodcutting — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-50 | Standard trees / Forestry events | Varlamore (Auburnvale) | Ents + various trees |
| 50-90 | Forestry events / Teak trees | Varlamore | Continue Forestry or Teaks for faster XP |
| 90-99 | Redwood trees | Kourend | Extremely AFK to 99. Best AFK WC method |

## Notes

- Redwoods (90 WC, 80 with Abundance boost) are the main reason Kourend is picked
- Forestry in Varlamore is solid for early-mid levels
- Logs from WC can feed into Construction (sawmill) and Fletching
- Abundance +10 boost = Redwoods accessible at 80 WC
