# Woodcutting — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Varlamore → Kourend

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~8 hrs |
| With Abundance | **~6.5 hrs** (+10 boost → Redwoods at 80 real instead of 90, skip slower methods) |
| With Woodsman | ~6.5 hrs (same WC time, but FM trains simultaneously for free) |

**Abundance saves ~1.5 hrs** (+10 boost = Redwoods 10 levels earlier).

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-51 real (61 eff) | Standard trees / Forestry events | Varlamore | **Stop here until Wintertodt.** 61 effective = can wield Dragon Axe if it drops from WT |
| Post-WT | Continue Forestry / Teaks with Dragon Axe | Varlamore | Use Dragon Axe if you got one |
| 80 real (90 eff) | Redwood trees | Kourend | Extremely AFK to 99. Best AFK WC method |

## Dependency: Wintertodt First

1. Train WC to **51 real (61 effective with Abundance)** — enough to wield Dragon Axe
2. Do Wintertodt (needs Kourend + rune axe + 40 FM real)
3. Dragon Axe drop (1/10k base, 1/2k at T4 5x drops) → equip at 61 effective
4. Continue WC to 80 real → Redwoods with Dragon Axe

Don't push WC past 51 real before WT — Dragon Axe makes everything after faster.

## Notes

- Redwoods (80 real with Abundance boost) are the main reason Kourend is picked
- Forestry in Varlamore is solid for early-mid levels
- Logs feed into Construction (sawmill) and Fletching
- With Woodsman relic: chopped logs auto-burn for FM XP (skip Wintertodt, but miss supply rewards)
