# Slayer — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kourend + Desert (barrage Slayer)

## Status: Waiting on Relic Info

May need a Slayer-specific relic if one is released. Watch daily reveals. Training Slayer normally in leagues (even with 5x Slayer points and 1/50 superior rate) could be slow without a dedicated relic/pact.

## Primary Method: Barrage Slayer in Catacombs

**Location:** Catacombs of Kourend (Kourend)
**Spellbook:** Ancient Magicks (Desert)
**Weapon:** Kodai Wand (CoX, Kourend)

- **5x Slayer reward points**
- **1/50 Superior Slayer** spawn rate (normally 1/200)
- 2x drop rates from eligible sources
- XP multiplier (5x → 16x)
