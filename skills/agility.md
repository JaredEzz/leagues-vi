# Agility — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kandarin, Desert, Varlamore

## Time Estimates

| Scenario | Est. Time to 99 |
|----------|-----------------|
| Base (16x, no relic) | ~14 hrs |
| With Abundance only | **~12 hrs** (Ardougne at 80 real, skip Pollnivneach) |
| With Abundance + Hotfoot | **0-4 hrs** (passive running XP or 2.8M/hr at Ardougne with 2x + 25%) |
| With Abundance + Woodsman | ~12 hrs (Woodsman doesn't affect Agility) |

**Abundance saves ~2 hrs** (+10 boost → Ardougne rooftop at 80 real instead of 90, skipping slower courses). The +2 XP per drop adds only +1.4% — nearly worthless for Agility since there are few XP drops per hour (~450).

**This is the slowest 99 in the maxing plan without Hotfoot.**

## Recommended Progression

1. **1-40 real:** Gnome (Ka) → Stepping Stones (V) — ~17 min
2. **40-50:** Colossal Wyrm Basic (V) — ~8 min
3. **50-60:** Seers' Village (Ka) — ~13 min
4. **60-80:** Pollnivneach (D) — ~1.5 hrs
5. **80-99:** Ardougne Rooftop (Ka) — **~9.9 hrs** (this is the grind)

## Notes

- Hallowed Sepulchre (Morytania) would be faster but region is locked
- Barbarian Fishing (Kandarin) also gives small passive Agility XP
- Abundance +10 = Ardougne at 80 real, Colossal Wyrm at 40 real
- Abundance +2/drop = +1.4% (negligible — Agility has too few drops/hr)
