# Agility — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Region:** Varlamore

## Primary Method: Colossal Wyrm Agility Course

**Location:** South of Civitas illa Fortis, Varlamore
**Requirement:** 50 Agility (40 with Abundance boost)

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-10 | Gnome Stronghold course | Kandarin | Quick start |
| 10-40 | Stepping stones (Yama's Lair) | Varlamore | Available from level 1 |
| 40-99 | Colossal Wyrm Agility Course | Varlamore | Best available course without Morytania (no Sepulchre) |

## Notes

- Hallowed Sepulchre (Morytania) would be faster but region is locked
- Colossal Wyrm is solid and entirely in starting area
- Barbarian Fishing (Kandarin) also gives passive Agility XP
- Abundance +10 = access to Colossal Wyrm at 40 Agility
