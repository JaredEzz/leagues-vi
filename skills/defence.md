# Defence — Skill Guide

**Target:** 99 | **Regions:** Kourend + Desert

## Methods

| Level Range | Method | Region | Notes |
|-------------|--------|--------|-------|
| 1-99 | Defensive casting with Ancient Magicks (Ice Barrage) in Catacombs | Ko + D | Trains Defence AND Magic simultaneously |

## Notes

- Barrage Slayer in Catacombs multi-combat = fastest Defence XP via defensive casting
- Dust Devils and Nechryaels are the best barrage tasks
- Requires Ancient Magicks (Desert unlock) + Catacombs (Kourend)
- Can also train Defence through melee Slayer if needed before Desert unlock
