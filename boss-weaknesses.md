## Devil's Element multiplier note

**Devil's Element** (off-hand from Echo Thermy, Kandarin) **doubles enemy elemental weaknesses**. With it equipped:
- Hueycoatl 60% → effective 120%
- Dark beast 60% → effective 120%
- Olm 50% → effective 100%
- Wardens 50% → effective 100%
- Ice Demon 150% → effective 300% (!!)

This is a *huge* force multiplier on the elemental DPS plan. Echo Thermy itself has no weakness, so farming it with raw mage damage + F8 is the path.
